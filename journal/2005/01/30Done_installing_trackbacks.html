Okay, that was relatively painless. To simplify things, I did have to change the permalinks of all my entries a very little bit. In the past, I had appended a number to the name of the entries, which would have uniquely identified them in case I had the same name entry but different extension. But I don't think I ever had that situation, so the unique identifier was pretty much superfluous. When I added the ability to view slices of my journal, those identifiers changed depending on what slice was being viewed. When I added the trackbacks, the permalinks would have been all flocked up. Even at the time I had created the unique IDs, they would be reshuffled if I added an entry between two existing entries.

So I'm cutting my losses and removing the unique ID.

And now I'm going to sleep again.
