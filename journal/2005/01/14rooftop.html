1:07am JST Saturday 15 January 2005

I invited Ikumi to explore the apartment building across the street. Its front door was no blocked with a gate; this is our big chance, I thought. The front door was not blocked because all the doors in the building were locked. Crud.

Peering around a bit, I noticed a ladder to the roof.

This is me kinda lodged inside the opening with a ladder to the roof.

Truly, it took me several minutes to convince myself (and Ikumi) that it would be okay for me to climb onto the roof for a quick picture. And truly, I wanted to slide down the slide looking thing, but we guessed it *might* not be designed for people to slide down. If I broke it, that would be a bit expensive.

I climbed onto the roof and she got pictures from the ground. With careful zooming, I can see my face (I was wearing all black) above the bottom end of the steps.
