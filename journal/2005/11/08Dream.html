9:27am IST Tuesday 08 November 2005

Explaining to my mom where I had been:

"I went to the nine inch nails concert over there," indicating with my finger, "and Fred came a bit later, and he wanted to talk to me about something I didn't want to talk about, so I ran up to my room and crawled underneath the bed and ate some of the pudding that I had left over.

"He came up but didn't find me, so I got up and I'm not sure how I got home; but I can't remember parking over here; I must have left Mr Van back there."

Ma didn't seem too impressed with my story, or any less upset that I had left Mr Van parked somewhere overnight, but she didn't get angry at me.
