State of My Life Address

  1. I am in Jerusalem, in the Israeli-occupied territory of Palestine, and have been here since October 6th. I plan to head back to Japan on 17 November.

Now my brain nearly wants to make up for lost time and somehow find a shiatsu teacher who will teach me free of charge in the 4 weeks I'll have free in Japan soon.
