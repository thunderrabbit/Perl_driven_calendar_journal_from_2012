14:26 IST Wednesday 16 November 2005

Hanging out with Niveen today!! We are en route to Bethlehem, which is good cause I have wanted to go...

She is shocked at the new settlements and the wall that has been built in the past 3 years since she was here last.

We still have to get through one checkpoint.

There is now a wall across the road where she used to go in.

"I am about to cry; oh God Rob why did they do this? I hate them. I hate them..."

Were her exact words. Now tell me that this wall doesn't bring more angst and anger to the Palestinians.

After a few minutes.. "They've split the city into pieces. This really sucks."

"Look at all these Israeli flags. I feel sick, Robert. They are so fast at destroying lies. They are so fast at building the wall."

"If I was living behind the wall, I would be the first person to bomb the Israelis."
