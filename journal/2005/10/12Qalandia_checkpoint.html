10:37am IST Wednesday 12 October 2005

On a bus to Nablus now. Well, on a bus to Qalandia which, if I am beginning to get a tiny grasp on anything I have experienced, is a checkpoint at which we will be requried to jump off the bus and walk through, then find a new form of transportation the rest of the way to Nablus.

I'm sitting next to Max, and Anna, who read "Qalandia" in Arabic on the bus, is in a nearby seat on this 19 seat bus.

10:57am

Wow. We just arrived at Qalandia, I just saw a painted silouhette of a young girl floating upward over the wall, lofted by balloons. It's a fine image in its on right, but even cooler that I've seen it online; apparently it was one of a series of paintings by a famous European graffiti artist Banksy.

In the article was a reference to a hidden word under rock or something; I'm interested to see if I can find that word now.

Except for the fact we are in a taxi to our next destination.
