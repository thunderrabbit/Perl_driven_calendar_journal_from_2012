8:49am IST Monday 24 October 2005

I had a dream about my long lost ex girlfriend Christine!

I was all affectionate and flirting with her; she responded physically, but then said, "don't do this; you're making me so *hot*," which she apparently didn't want to be. I hypothesized she wasn't single, and therefore didn't want to hook up with me.

- - - -

Racing downstairs to use the restroom, I realized it would be faster to fly. I flew over the door, through a glass window protected by a grate, and then swooped into the restroom. I knew that I could pee, but that for some reason it wouldn't actually help. However, as a visual joke, I decided to pee myself backwards, using it like a liquid rocket in some crazy movie (*).

I peed and flew backwards out of the room and backwards back down the hall, racing my friend back. He couldn't come too close for fear of being pissed on. I won the race!

(*) if this has been done in a movie, I don't know about it.
