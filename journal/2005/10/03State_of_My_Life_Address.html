State of My Life Address

  1. I am nearly ready to leave Japan for Palestine. I don't really know what I'm getting myself into, and I'm a bit nervous, but mostly happy/excited/optimistic.

  2. I have no girlfriend, no pets, no car, no house/apt, no idea what I will do after PB 52nd voyage, and very little idea what I will do between Palestine and P/B.

  3. I am living at Kellie's house and have been for the last month, basically. I leave on Wednesday (2 days away).

  4. I've gotten all 33 tapes from the 49th P/B voyage transcribed, but I haven't gotten all my photos incorporated into my journal. I'm running out of days, but I really want to do that still; I am sure the importance will plummet once I'm in Palestine.

  5. I have been playing ultimate frisbee for the past three Sundays on the far end of the baseball fields near Futakotamagawa station. I enjoyed playing and meeting cool people. I look forward to playing again once I get back in Tokyo.

  6. I've downloaded Skype, and have enjoyed talking to Niveen free of charge, and to Fred very cheaply.

  7. I've been talking to Niveen who has assured me many times that everything will be fine; she's given me advice on what to wear and what to bring.

  8. Just today I received the PIN for my credit card so I can get cash out while in Palestine.

  9. I enjoyed a great okonomiyaki food-antics party at Millenium House last night with Jen, Shoko, Ikumi, Cyrus, Keiko, and others who were randomly in and out of the room.

  10. I haven't had a proper job in months, and my bank accounts are showing it. I've got basically $1200 (US) to my name. That's supposed to last until April 2006. Good thing my return flight is paid.

  11. Supposed to meet Hitomi tomorrow; she will hold my summer clothes so I can have them for the 52nd voyage, but not worry about them until then.

  12. I have released my skates and helmet. I feel a bit bad for Ikumi who held them for me while I was on the 49th voyage. But I'm sure that was easier for her than releasing them was for me.
