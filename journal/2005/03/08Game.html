9:38am JST Tuesday 08 March 2005

Dan and I played a new game today.

One person lies on his/her back, looking directly up at the light string-pull thing, the other person raises then drops the string toward the lier's face. The game: don't flinch.

We each did poorly at first, but got better. On his last attempt, Dan mockingly cringed like he was about to be flattened by a sumo wrestler.

We don't yet have a name for the game.
