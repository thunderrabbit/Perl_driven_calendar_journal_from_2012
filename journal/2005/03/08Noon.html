Went to a local bike shop to buy a new back wheel for Mimibike. The guy basically said it would be less expensive to buy a new bike (Mimibike is a US made bike, so he'd have to order new stuff) so we talked about using a hammer to BHANG the wheel back into shape, but he settled on just tweaking the spokes' tension to fix it. The bend was lateral, not vertical.

The wheel is amazingly straight now! Only 200 yen; less than the price of a small lunch.
