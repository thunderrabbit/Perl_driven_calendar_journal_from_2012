8:55am AST Wednesday 15 June 2005

Today we have arrived in Al-Aqaba Jordan. Last night between 10pm and 11pm we could see lights on both sides of the ship as we sailed into the small isthmus (is that the word?) at the top of the Red Sea.

I hung out with the Sri Lankan students at their final party in Sports Bar; it was a low key event, which I appreciated: not too much smoking, and plenty of space on the dance floor. Though I didn't really dance.

This morning I've got the Indiana Jones theme music in my head: we are going to see Petra, where they filmed the final scene of the third movie... I've always wanted to visit this place (since that movie?), and this is the single tour that I decided 100% I would do. From the first time Audrey said we'd get to go.

I'm planning to wear cargo pants (so I can carry extra batteries and tapes and Colleen's rabbit (Beanie Bunny could become his name)) and long sleeved shirt. I gotta go get my hat from the GET office, and I hope I can find the small bag Hitomi suggested I bring. Though I can just carry my backpack again.

Last night I copied all the videos from my camera to computer, and burned them all to CD (245 megs). Also burned the remaining pictures that I hadn't archived yet.

I'm well-behind my tape transcription. I'm (not really) typing Tape 3, and recording on Tape 7. I'll take Tape 8, ready to go in Jordan.
