9:29pm AST Thursday 16 June 2005

I just said bye to the Sri Lankan students, and secretly I kinda gave up on learning all their names. I couldn't keep the syllables straight in my head. I know four names, well five. I know three of the guys and two of the girls names. I just ran briefly off the boat to give them my yellow name cards.

I saw one of the guys at the door and was like, "can you give these to the Sri Lankan students?" cause they were standing right there at the bottom of the gangplank. He was like, "aw yeah, go ahead.."

"What?" ... "kickass!!" so I got to run off the boat *after* the absolute deadline for getting on the boat! Whooooo!!

10:15pm

We are leaving the Gulf of Aqaba. Lots of screams and "we love you!" and "I can't believe we are leaving!!" People are in huge spirits, chattering at hundreds of meters per second. Now Jasna, Eilat, and Niveen are on the boat. Jasna has been on the boat many times; she is the person in charge of coordinating meetings and stuff for Eilat and Niveen. Eilat is from Israel and Niveen is from Palestine.
