11:16pm AST Wednesday 15 June 2005

I just bought two sand containers: one for Fred and one for my mom. I considered getting one for janette, but decided these tapes will be enough.

The guy named an amazing price for putting names inside the jars, so I was like, "aight" and eager to see how he could possibly position each sand grain to spell out a letter, much less a name! Holy crap, how could it be possible for only $4??? The economy must be realllly depressed here in Jordan. It's a steal!

He got out his pocketknife and dug out the sand from the top of the jar I had selected. Then, in a quick deft of incredible sand-positioning skill, he took a little toothpick, dabbed some glue on it and wrote the name backwards inside the jar. He shook some sand around and that was it. Bingo: one name written in sand inside the jar.

Welp, he didn't say he would postion each grain, and it certainly is a good use of glue, and a better use of time than doing it the way I was thinking.
