12pm AST Wednesday 15 June 2005

Just bought a 60 minute cassette tape for 1 dinar. Got three coins back, but don't know anything about them yet. I was really happy with the transaction. I had been scared to even approach anyone, being an American in Jordan, but everybody was normal and I showed them the type of cassette I wanted. The guy didn't have one, but took me to a nearby shop that did have them and explained what I wanted. They didn't have a huge selection, but pulled out a tape and I pulled out my money. I have no idea the value of dinars, but 1 was enough. I used the three remaining coins to buy some banana chips.
