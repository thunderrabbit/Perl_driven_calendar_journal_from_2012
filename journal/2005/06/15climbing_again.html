Upon arrival at the hotel, I climbed onto the roof of our room. The rooms were arranged in stairstep rows as if we were hampsters living in stairs of a staircase. Therefore it was trivial to go up the stairs between the rooms and walk onto the roof of the room below. I sat with Rie and a couple of others on the edge of the roof, watching the sunset playing shadows on the tall rock backdrop.

Though the pebbles on the roof were not exactly feather soft, we took a nap up there for a bit.
