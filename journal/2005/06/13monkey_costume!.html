12:26am AST Tuesday 14 June 2005

Awesome 飲みほだい fundraiser costume party tonight! Most awesome was my costume which I found in the costume room: a blue long sleeve security guard shirt, red and yellow vertical striped baggy clown pants, and monkey ears!! Best monkey costume ever!

I totally loved being in character: I galloped around with a pitcher of beer to pour for people; I grabbed food off the trays and ate with my hands; I picked and ate lice from peoples' hair; I sniffed and snarfed around some of the other characters and generally just made a cute nuisance of myself. I decided I'd like to be a monkey at every costume party I attend.

Especially if I can climb on more things.

On the top deck, I did a *bit* of climbing; crouching on chairs and leaping off them, though I had to be careful because they move quite easily on the rough plastic surface.

Several people wanted to take pictures with me, and I was happy to do so. For several of the initial photos, I did huge toothy grin and squinty eyes, but later decided that it just made me look like I was in seething pain or really angry.
