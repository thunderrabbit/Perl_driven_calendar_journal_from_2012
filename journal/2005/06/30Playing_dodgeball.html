8:30pm CEST Thursday 30 June 2005

Just played dodgeball with a volleyball up on the top deck. Fun times, though the Japanese rules are different than what I'm used to. They use only one ball at a time and catching the ball does not make the thrower out. Getting hit by the ball forces a player to the edges of the opposite side of the court, and if that player ends up with a ball and can hit someone on the other team, that player is resurrected.

Now it's time for Niveen and Eilat's lecture. They have barricaded the door to make it look like a checkpoint. I saw it, ran down to get my tape recorder, came up and found I still was not late. The line hadn't moved at all.
