5:52pm AST Thursday 16 June 2005

We have arrived back at the ship and then left the ship again.. Hitomi and I are walking from the ship toward the center of town to see the huge flag and the sea and whatever else we can find.

This city seems like it has such potential for huge growth. The boulevard is new yet not crowded with traffic; there are lots of lots with some under construction. Even old houses are still being used. The Red Sea is utterly beautifully fantastic. If nothing else, I could just get scuba training license and teach people to dive here. If there were enough wannabe divers, I guess.

For some reason I was quite taken with the ice cream and its wrapper.
