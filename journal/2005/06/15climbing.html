4:04pm AST Wednesday 15 June 2005

This sorta looks like a face in the mountains. Despite the filename, I think it's in Wadi Rum, not Petra.

I climbed barefootedly up a narrow chasm between two sets of rocks and several people took pictures of me and Francois, my newly named rabbit. I enjoyed the sudden attention, but was a bit worried that I would get in trouble, either by simply being where I wasn't supposed to be, by being barefoot and having someone complain, or by having someone climb after me and get hurt in the process.

Fortunately, none of these things happened, and I actually climbed to a point remote enough that I couldn't see the end of the line for all the others on the tour.

On the way down, I tried to go quickly so people wouldn't think I was a wuss, but I had to be careful so I wouldn't end up falling and breaking my skull.

Careful descent worked, but then on the very (very!) last step I kinda miscalculated and hit the bottom of my foot in a painful way. But... no blood: no problem.

I staged two photos of Francois doing his own rock climb.

This is Raed, our tourguide. He's from the US and was pretty entertaining. He seemed to know answers to every question we asked him.
