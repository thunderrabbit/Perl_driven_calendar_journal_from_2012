8pm AKDT Tuesday 16 August 2005

We were interrupted by whale sightings several times, and most of them were boring: Way off in the distance, we see a little bit of spray and then watch the spine slowly slide by and then the tail flips up and shoops straight down and the whale is gone. Well, underwater.

BUT, one time we were all climbing on couches with noses pressed against the windows and this whale JUMPED out of the water did a half turn and kerspalooshed into the water!! Yah - that was cool.
