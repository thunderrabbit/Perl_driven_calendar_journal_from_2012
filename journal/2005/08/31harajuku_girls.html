Oh my gosh I am seeing all these Harajuku Girls (as noted in Gwen Stefani's song) wearing little cat ears and giant boots and frilly lacey goth-French-maid outfits. Stuffed rabbit necklace pendants, tight shredded pants, necktie worn as a belt, streaky bleachie white hair, lip piercing, black oversized hats on sideways. The fashion here is just so confused: rap, hip hop, goth, cos-play, etc, all combined.
