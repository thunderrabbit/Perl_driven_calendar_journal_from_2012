pm ANAST Friday 26 August 2005

Had a heart-to-heart with Kattrean, who revealed that she has even another layer behind what I thought was the hard shell and then the soft interior: the calculating Kattrean that decides if it's important to take care of other people or not, in terms of what relationship she ultimately wants with them.

She said she was no longer willing to just give me what I want, partially because she wants something more than just what we've had so far. I'm kinda scared by it; why wasn't she honest in the first place? Or was she? She was like, "if I didn't care, I know you'll be gone in a few days, so I would just give you what you want" (in this case a shoulder for crying/bitching about Hitomi) ", but that wouldn't be the real me, which has some needs as well."
