1:55pm JST Tuesday 30 August 2005

Up on the port deck now watching the rest of the passengers yell over the rail before they leave and get one more day of food and fun on the ship.

I got my stuff takubined to Kellie's house, though they wouldn't guarantee what day it will arrive, much less what time. But it was cheap.

Kuni asked me to throw something to the ship: a bag of nuts attached to a sturdy ribbon of plastic. I was like, "aight," figuring I can whirl it around my head and release at the right time to send it on its way.

I am *sure* it would have made it, but one of Kuni's friends stepped on the inside of the ribbon, so it got stuck on his leg and just stopped in mid-trajectory and fell.

Tetsuya, the cruise director saw me from the bridge deck and gave me a big batsu (X shape with his forearms). I hope I haven't mucked my chances to sail again..
