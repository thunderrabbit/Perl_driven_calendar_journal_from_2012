8:30pm AKDT Friday 19 August 2005

This is the last port. We handed out champagne and watched the port go away. Funny is that we were right next to a huge ship and we all waved to them and they all waved to us, and there was some kind of cruise-ship connection, plus we were all emotionally high on this being our last port.

I chilled with Naho in a heated area of heat being dumped out from the lower decks.
