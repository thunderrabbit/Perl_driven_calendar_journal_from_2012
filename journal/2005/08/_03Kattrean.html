11:32pm CDT Wednesday 03 August 2005

Today has been a lovely day in Acapulco! I'm bunking out with Kattrean in a cool hotel on the Senor Frog's side of the bay, near Wal Mart. The hotel is a family run place; there are maybe five suites, each with A/C and ceiling fan in the master bedroom (one king size bed, restroom with large shower), A/C and ceiling fan in the kids' bedroom (two double beds, restroom with large shower), and no AC, but ceiling fans in the kitchenette and living room area with balcony. All for only 450 pesos per night (about US $45), and check out time is 4pm.

Kattrean has stuffed my mouth with 16 grapes and poured some alcohol down in between them. One should only have alcohol poured over 16 grapes in one's mouth if one can swallow with one's mouth open.

We had big fun playing house, visiting the 24 Hour Walmart and buying food, both good and junk, plus buying condoms, in Spanish, trying to work out which ones had spermicide (so we wouldn't buy them cause she is allergic).

Kattrean and I stayed at a hotel called Katalina, while Angie and Ryan stayed at a hotel called Angelita. How perfect!
