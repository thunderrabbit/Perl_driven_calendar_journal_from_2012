6:45am JST Tuesday 30 August 2005

Last night I offered up my beloved (well worn) boat shoes up to anyone who might want them. I couldn't bear to throw them away, so I put them on the Emergency Escape Breathing Device (which I don't know how to use) near my room. This morning I found two cigarettes crushed out into the shoes, so I guess someone decided they were trash. I felt a bit sad, but I offered them up as a gift to the universe, and if that's how they were to be treated, so be it.
