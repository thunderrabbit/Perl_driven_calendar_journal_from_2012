3:45pm AKDT Tuesday 16 August 2005

Katt and I went to Yacht Club for teatime and there was a long ass line going all the way out the door and down the stairs. I hypothesized that the little snacks given at teatime couldn't have suddenly become *that* popular, so I bypassed the line to see what was up. People were queued to get cups of whiskey coffee. Absolutely unbelievable what things are popular in various cultures. I hate coffee and I hate whiskey, so I did not join the line.
