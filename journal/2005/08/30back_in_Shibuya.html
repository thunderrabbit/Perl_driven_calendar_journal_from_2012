8:11pm JST Tuesday 30 August 2005

Wow; has it really been 2 days since I changed my clock?

Katt and I have been wandering around Shibuya, starting with El Torito and then ice cream and now chillin' in a KFC during a rain storm.

We saw a passenger from the boat, and we saw Olivia randomly in Shibuya station! We talked to her about ten minutes and did a quick version of catchup.. Olivia is working hard and Jen is in Bali for a quick visa reset. I didn't ask about Tetsuya nor Dan...

Katt and I found a club called Christ On, which had a biblical motif and is probably a goth club. They had a door (and Katt said therefore a cover charge) so we didn't go in.

I fell asleep several times on the way from Nihonbashi to Shibuya despite my intention to tell Katt stories of the various stations... I didn't even budge for Tsunashima nor Motosumiyoshi.

It's so weird to see Japanese people and think "they must be from the ship!" but then realize, hey - everyone around us is Japanese!
