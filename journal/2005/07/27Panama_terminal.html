1:45pm EST Wednesday 27 July 2005

We're docked in Cristobol, Panama now, which has been described as so dangerous that we must not leave the terminal. This rule has been set down as law for volunteers and paid staff, but the paying passengers are allowed to exit the terminal. Apparently there were several muggings last time, though.

Several booths are set up inside the terminal, hawking trinkets and T-shirts and crap that are (from my point of view) barely distinguishable from most of the other ports we've visited. There is an internet cafe with three computers, five flat screen monitors, and several empty desks. $2 per half hour.

I wrote a four page letter to Kellie on the Rody stationery that she sent to Jamaica for me! Okay; I'm going to write some emails to people and maybe send them from this port.
