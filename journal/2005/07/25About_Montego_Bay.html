(written 12:01am JST Tuesday 26 April 2005)

It seems that Montego Bay is a mishmash of cultures, mostly financed via tourism, with probably a decent amount via drug trade. (I glean this last bit from a site whose Hot Tip about the area was to remember that drug posession and use is illegal)

I am warned to beware of pickpockets while I'm encouraged to bask in the glorious sun on any of the fine beaches. On one map a railway across the country is marked with Out Of Service written along it.
