5:35pm JST Friday 01 April 2005

I'm using gmail as my main email storage. rob@robnugen.com is my address and thunderrabbit@gmail.com is my storage. And, as I'm typing, my gmail storage quota is going up. It was 1000 MB yesterday. It was 1125 MB a moment ago. Now it's 1145 MB. Gmail guys have a counter on the front of their site.

Oh my god, the counter matches my quota.

Gmail rules.

On the front page of gmail.com there is a counter spinning up about 10k per second. That's how much storage we have on our accounts. When I click refresh on my gmail screen, the percent used thing matches how much it says we are allowed. Right now it's at 1149 megabytes.

I am guessing that it will top out at 2000 MB at the end of April 1st California time and then slowly inch its way up from there. I don't care to do the calculation to see if that makes sense, but, that's my guess.
