Dan's in town! It was really great to see him come through my door; I had forgotten at that minute that he would be coming, but I knew in general he would come today. Hitomi was totally surprised.

We all went outside to enjoy some drinks and pudding. Jackie had come down to chat, so she and Jen gabbed while Dan and I played cricket. After the game, I was a bit hungry and whipped up a batch of broken corn chips and mozarella cheese in the microwave.

Katrina came down after beating her goal of sleeping until 5pm and the girls talked about girl stuff while Dan journaled and I sat quietly listening, but not really paying attention; I was focused on cheese and crackers.

I'm headed to Yokohama to meet Hitomi for Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind, which I've seen twice, but enjoyed it enough to see it again (and pay for it this time). Kinda a reward to Jim and Kate.
