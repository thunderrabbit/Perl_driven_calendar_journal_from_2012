11:16am JST Thursday 21 April 2005

During the process of cleaning out my room, I decided I would not keep the jacket Jon gave me before I came to Japan. I emailed him to see if he wanted it back, and he said I should mail my mom; she had given it to me.

She said that she *brought* it from Texas to Japan when they came in November 2003, and I never thanked her for it. Fuckin' hell. I have a completely different memory, and am sad to imagine that I never thanked her and that for all this time she has felt unappreciated around carrying the jacket over. Damn.

So I decided the best thing I could do is take some pictures of me appreciating the jacket and sending those to her. And offer the option of sending the jacket back to her or giving it away here.
