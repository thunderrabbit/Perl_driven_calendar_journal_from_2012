Ryan North is a really cool guy. He's been writing the incredible Dinosaur Comics for years. Maybe centuries.

Ryan North is a humble man. He knows he can't draw. So the comic image is always the same.

How does one take this to the next level of comical creativity??

Maybe we could use the *INTERNET* in this endeavor!

The result is Whispered Apologies, a comic where we create the comics without words and Ryan and his gang add the words.

So I took some pics, cropped them and used 

montage -geometry 640x480+10+10 -bordercolor white -label "" cartoon*.* rob_nugen1.jpg

to string them together.

Magic!
