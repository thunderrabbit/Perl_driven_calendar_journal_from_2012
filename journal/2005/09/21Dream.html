10:20am JST Wednesday 21 September 2005

I was on Pb's next voyage, and going to some of the same places which now began to feel like home. We were also going to some new places: Nicaragua and elsewhere.

I was in the pool up on the 8th floor, but it was in a room like in a hotel. After I came down for dinner, a whole line of people were going to the pool; it was like the line of Sneeches going to get stars, but they were going to get in the pool, and then leaving just as quickly.

I didn't understand why I wasn't aware of the schedule, and hoped I wasn't missing anything.
