11-ish pm JST Friday 2 September 2005

Kattrean and I are sitting outside the New Zealand Embassy.

She was like, "why did you cry?" after I cried at one point.

I was like, "cause I don't know why you're one more person who says, 'I don't know why I love you; I just do.'"

She goes, "maybe that's because it's your love language and you need to hear it."

Perhaps, to stop the pattern of people telling me they love me, but don't know why, I need to love myself maybe.

Perhaps Kiwis have a more positive outlook on life because they are thinking about the group, and what's best for the group. Therefore they see what's good in other people and it makes the world a happier place.

Americans think about what's in their own best interest, and maybe therefore don't see what's best in other people. Therefore they see the bad in people and it makes the world ucky.

11:40pm JST Friday 2 September 2005

Leaving the embassy now. Kattrean basically said she will respond to whatever emails I send, but she won't send first because I am basically breaking up with her.

Other than that, it was a fine conversation. Basically we wondered the wonders of the universe. We said "I love you today" for a while on the boat. Today she was like, "why would you say 'today' - what will change tomorrow?" and I was like, "well, I might not be willing to love you tomorrow."

She goes, "well, if people love you, why would they not be willing to do stuff for you the next day?"

"I don't know."

"We might not solve this mystery tonight."

- - -

I said, "welp, see ya soon," and gave her a kiss on the cheek.

She goes, "well, given what we've said tonight, I don't know about that."

"Well, smell ya never," and I just watched her walk away.

She opened the door at the gate, and she did not look back as she went in. She had the white flower in her hair. Beautiful hair. Beautiful Kattrean.

Just too short. God dammit.

- - - -

It took me a while to say this, but I was like, "welp; I won; I walked away first."

After I told her I had to break up because she was too short, and possibly other reasons, she was like, "why would I tell someone something I don't like about them? to make them change? I don't want you to change; I love you they way you are." (*)

I'm like, "stop that shit; I'm unloveable; why would I say 'I can't date you and can't tell you why?'" and I don't know if it's the right thing or not, but that's what happened.

(*) Which I actually think is untrue. She loves the version of me that doesn't lie and respects sex as only in the one relationship.
