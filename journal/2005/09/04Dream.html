8:12am JST Sunday 04 September 2005

Reading TIME magazine I saw a 2-page layout advertising 6 books for sale. The books didn't seem like all that amazing save that they were being pimped by TIME. I could just imagine the cash suddenly flowing in for these authors and I felt so crushingly depressed that I have not written a book yet. Then I felt a wave of "get over it; just write a book," and I felt all better then woke up gently.

Should I write about my adventures on P.B.? Should I learn Japanese then research and write the history of P.B.?
