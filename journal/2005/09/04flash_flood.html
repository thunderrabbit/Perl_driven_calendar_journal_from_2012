11:34pm JST Sunday 04 September 2005

Kellie and I walked to the grocery store to get some foodage and discovered significant rain flooding the streets! We waited in vain for it to subside, watching gushing gutters squirt water that slowly rose toward the entrance of the store. I was unimpressed when the store security guard and some low level employee put down bags of rocks in front of less than half of the encroaching line of water! Hadn't they seen the levee in New Orleans??

I enjoyed watching the flooded creek, but didn't enjoy getting wet.
