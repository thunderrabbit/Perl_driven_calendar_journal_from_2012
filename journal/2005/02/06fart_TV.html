Japanese TV has some strange shows.

I don't know if I'd classify this as a game show, and I'm not sure what market they're trying to reach, but... here is the setup:

Five male comedians, two pairs and a solo act, go to a sushi bar. Not cheap kaiten sushi, but an expensive place. They'd like to eat sushi, right?

Here's the rule: a sushi serving is served to a guy if he can fart.

Each time one of them farts, the farter gets a serving of sushi.

Cut a fart, get sushi.

They've got microphones behind each man's stool (no pun intended) and a camera watching from the ceiling and one or two guys walking around with cameras.

Each time one of them feels a fart coming, they signal so the cameras can watch his ass while the TV subtitle says "屁コミング" [fart coming].

A guy in the background does a celebratory drum roll each time a fart is achieved.
