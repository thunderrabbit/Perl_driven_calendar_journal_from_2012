8:15am JST Friday 25 February 2005

I was with Laura from Castle of Dreams and I had climbed the large tree in the middle of the living room, to look for Thunder Rabbit's old burrow. I found it, but the tree had grown around it. I tried to uncover it and scads of rotten wood came pouring from the tree into the living room. Like 1000 cubic yards of mess gushed forth. My mom was not impressed, and I wasn't sure how I could clean it up.
