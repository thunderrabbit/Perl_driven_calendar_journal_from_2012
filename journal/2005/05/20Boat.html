The picture here is of most of the female GET Teachers: From left to right: Nicole, Kim, Angie, Kris, Maryann, Carissa, and Natalie (asleep).

We've just chowed lunch out on the short-grass covered hill on what Kim suggests feels like an island. We're on the port overlooking the boat. I'm all snotty nosed cause I think the interior of the boat is dusty. Uh oh.

Met Katrina (from Poland) and Amelia (from Bulgaria), bar staff onboard. I felt an inch of seasick(?) inside the boat; combination of tight space and a bit of rocking. Carissa seems to be quite sensitive to the rocking.

There are 8 levels on the boat (I think), including the upper deck. The lower decks don't always have openings for fresh air, which is why a couple of guys were like, "don't forget to go outside."

It's like living in an enclosed city, protected from the elements outside, trapped with the elements inside.

Gotta go back in soon. Maybe to teach.

At most ports the boat gets touchup paint.
