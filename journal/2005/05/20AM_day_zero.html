10:07am JST Friday 20 May 2005

Took several pictures of me with my bags and Millenium House and a couple of shots while on the train, mostly of Motosumiyoshi station construction. Arrived well on-time to this beautiful port building where the key design point was "no vertical lines" combined with "no visible support system"

I've been here before, but it was quieter, and random strangers didn't say Good Morning to me.

Oh oh oh, coming up to the building, I saw the ship, and it looked different than I thought: older and squatter, maybe. Took some pix and continued, then saw OUR boat, or what I thought must be our boat could I could see CE on the smoke stack which would be the end of "PEACE"...

Walked up alongside the squat boat, and confirmed it was not ours. Got a few shots of The Topaz, my new home for the next three months.

An elderly Japanese guy took a shot of me with the boat.

Waved HI to the guys working busily on the bow; they waved "hello" back, and I said I would see them in an hour.
