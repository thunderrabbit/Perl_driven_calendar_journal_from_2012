10:08am

In the van now for our tour; I get to sit by Kim. Dude - Vietnam is beautiful. And poor. After hearing the story of 1000s of years of war; it's fucked up, but that's what it is. Right now we've got hills and banana trees on the left side and the sea on our right. People walking around with triangle hats. This road is two well-paved lanes each direction leading to and from the port. We all got a triangle hat.

There's construction happening among dilapidated buildings, including a house with walls made of straw mats. These roads are less than 8 years old. Because Danang is on the sea and has four beaches, Americans come here and bring their money; this is the wealthiest city in Vietnam, and it looks like hell. Dude. It fuckin' looks like hell.

There are no crosswalks nor traffic lights, but plenty of bicycles and pedestrians and motorcycles.

Most men are wearing baseball hats with no logo and women wear either triangle hats, baseball hats, or gilligan hats.
