6:30pm JST Thursday 12 May 2005

I wrote this and had Excite's translator translate it for Hitomi.

For some reason I have been unable to cleanly break away from you. I
have tried telling you to get all your stuff out of my room. I have
tried hitting you. I have tried telling you I hate you. I have tried
to jump through my window. But you always stay.

I was looking forward to having some free time away from Hitomi while
I was on PB.

When we are normal, and don't live together in my room, I enjoy seeing
you more often. I look forward to hearing from you. For example,
yesterday I called you a few times to see where you were; I was
expecting to hang out with you and was surprised when you weren't here
by around 8pm. I was looking forward to seeing you yesterday evening.

I was looking forward to my own time today. Today I planned to clean
my room, carefully archiving things and creating a scrap book. But
instead we are fighting and I'm destroying my room and chair and
fingers and not creating a scrap book, but talking to you about the
same things we always talk about and I always hate talking about and
always tell you I don't want to talk about it.

I don't want to spend time on a relationship with any woman right
now. Not with you, Satoko, Ikumi, etc. But I do like sex. That desire
attracts me to you and it seems to bring nothing but trouble. I should
just go fuck a prostitute and be done with it.

I want to work on my own life. I want to find some interesting project
in my life. I was hoping that PB would afford me the
opportunity to find such a project. maybe I will fall in love with
Singapore or Sri Lanka or sailing and want to do more. Maybe I will
fall in love with PB and want to live in Tokyo longer so I can
work for PB.

I would respect you if you seemed to have your own life. If you have
your own things to do. If you were less often available to me. Don't
wait for me all the time. It's a waste of your life to wait for me. I
am not available for you. Don't be available for me. That will make me
more interested in seeing you.
