We're in Hong Kong! It was really weird to look outside and see...

A Building! outside the ship and not like water and waves and nothing. And we ain't movin'.. it's like "hey man, why are we stopped?" Oh, I remember - "we're in Hong Kong!"

So that's kinda weird, but..

Then I'm like, "I can record it!" but, I didn't have my recorder, so I had to go back and get it, and for some strange reason I had to actually pause and think, "okay, get back on the boat; you'll be really upset if you don't go get it."

So now, I can walk through this hallway and be like, "hey the shops are closed!"

I was even smart enough to bring an extra tape, though I wasn't smart enough to bring an extra battery. But, we're in Hong Kong! Can buy new batteries. Changed US$20 to HK$150 and they say an internet cafe is about HK$20, and I'm like "that's enough for me"

I'm gonna take Star Ferry to get outta here. I found Shawn; we're trying to escape this shopping mall. We're docked right next to a huge shopping mall.
