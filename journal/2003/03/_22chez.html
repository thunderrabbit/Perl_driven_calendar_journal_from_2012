Chez and I seem to find ourselves closer to one another.. like two souls with common paths.

"You seem to be similar to me in that... lots of people... want to have you pay attention to them."

From the previous bit of conversation, I knew what she was going to say by the first ellipses.

I'm enjoying our connection. I'm trying not to covet it. That's the perfect word, I imagine.
