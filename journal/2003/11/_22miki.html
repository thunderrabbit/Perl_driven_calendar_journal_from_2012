Today, Miki texted me that she took an onsen three times yesterday. A little twitch happened in my mind, a realization that we are not merged; she still has her own life and does her own things... and I began to break up with her. In fact, I almost broke it off altogether and shut her out of my life. I think this revelation could not have come soon enough, although it still hurt a bit to recognize this truth.

This "truth" has been given to me by Dr Wayne Dyer (I think), or whover wrote the chapter on Love in The Road Less Traveled, I think.

We fall in love and it feels like we've merged with the partner and it feels infinite. Then we slowly realize that we are separate people as the illusion wears off and we have the chance to actively love one another or break up. Or some combination platter, of course.
