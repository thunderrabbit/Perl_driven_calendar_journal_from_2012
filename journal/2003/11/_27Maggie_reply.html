My Dear Friend.  I feel like crushes are the some of the best
motivators that a person can have.  I find that they have motivated me
to do better work, to look better, to take better care of myself, to
act with great integrity, to impress another on every level.  They
come and they go.  Some seem to stay much longer than I could dream
they would.  (Because they migrate more fully to what I call falling
in love.)  These are some of the best feelings that life has to offer.

I can only name one that I find better - I would call it being in love
with God and being in love with myself.  And I think I also calling
that "Acting in Right" or more simply "Acting with Integrity."  I love
myself so much more when I know that the actions I am taking have no
smudge of shame with them because it would be alright with me if every
person in the entire world knows what I am choosing.  This leads me to
act in a way that is most loving to every person in my life and to
myself and to God.  Tough act ot follow?  Sure.  But I feel like if I
don't have a high standard, then I end up with a mediocre standard and
then I become mediocre.

Alright.  It sounds like you already know what I just wrote.  There's
nothing wrong with _feeling_ affection for any person.  There's
