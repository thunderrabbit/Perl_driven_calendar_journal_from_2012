8:10am JST Tuesday 4 November 2003

It was within the last week I believe, that Tomoko was in voice with me. I was like bingbong! when she said she loved roller coasters with her big smile and perfect teeth. Nice.

If not for all these other students I'd give you my address..

I commented to Bill that I met this girl in voice and I want her digits.

"I thought you have a girlfriend."

"I do, but I can still want to talk to others. I'm not trying to bang her; I just want to ride roller coasters with her."

I kept monitoring the voice room between classes and during my break to see if she was still there.

I had a class with Yuuya (fun little kid with big eyes due to his farsighted-fixin' glasses) and then went out front and.. whoah there was Tomoko leaving.

Other students were in the area but Japanese culture will generally mind its own business. The staff were engaged with students and there were no other teachers around.

you only get one shot do not miss your chance ...

I got a piece of paper from my calendar-book and wrote my email for her.

"What are you writing?" she asked, which sounded to me like, "are you writing something for me?"

"My address. Can you read it?"

She didn't get what I was asking, or she didn't answer clearly yes/no, but she took the paper and my pen. Then Yuuya came up and part of me was like, "eeep!" but then I realized no worries; he's not spying on me, he's playing with me.

So I gave him five, and he put up his other hand and I gave him five again back and forth on each hand [five] [five] [five] [five] [five] [five] basically ignoring Tomoko while she wrote. Didn't say anything else to her and almost didn't look at her while she handed me the paper and my pen back.

Mission accomplished.

I had to check my brain as I went back to the teachers lounge. Inside I was flying.
