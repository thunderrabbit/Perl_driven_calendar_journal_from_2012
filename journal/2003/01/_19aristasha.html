9:37am CST Sunday 19 January 2003

During closing circle I saw where Marta was and wondered if I could do Aristasha and not get flubbed up if I saw her. She was positioned in the circle where the sun was all bright when I looked that direction, so I left my eyes closed for that part of the circle as I rotated around in the center.

Before I did Aristasha as all the youth were hollering "we love you!" type stuff, Jared (I think) goes, "I want your baby!" and that kinda flipped me out, but I pretended to ignore it and just go with Aristasha.

As random people were saying bye to me at the very end, she came up and and said bye and good luck. Touched my left arm. Some old woman kinda interrupted. I'll take that as a blessing, but my ego wanted to make sure things were fixed with Marta, or to know what was in her mind, and know if she got the pics of Chucky's brown belt test.

I wish her the best.
