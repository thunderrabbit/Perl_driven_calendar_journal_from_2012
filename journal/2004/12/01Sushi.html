Daniel and I went to kaiten sushi after we arrived back in Motosumiyoshi. The train was carrying plates around the bar, but the plates were not carrying sushi. I was like, "no no,put the plate over there" so it would be like kaiten sushi, instead of him just handing me the plate. He had to tell the guys in the back not to toss the sushi. Some crazy foreigner wants his sushi to ride the train. Funny.
