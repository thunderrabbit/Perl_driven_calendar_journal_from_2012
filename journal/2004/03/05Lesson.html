12:19pm JST Friday 5 March 2004

I was late to my Japanese lesson today. I called Ayana but she didn't answer. I told Ayana this and she was like, "really?" and I checked. Oops; I had called Akina.

I was able to read from the book kinda sorta fast, after a slow pass or two. Partially I had memorized the words, and not-so-much linked the kanji to words in my mind. But progress was made even so.

After the lesson, that cute girl who is always behind the counter when I arrive was not behind the counter, but chillin' outside against the rail next to the street. Ayana was like, "you want to talk to her; I gotta go."

I was like, "おなまえは。。" and she's like, "ゆり" though actually she said a long something and then "yuri. Nice to meet you. はじめまして。"

I told her my name and she didn't get it the first time so I wrote it on the trunk of the nearby motorcycle with my finger, and kinda scratched the trunk's sunfaded plastic surface, and I was nervous and misspelled it (in katakana, I was like "ラブ wait, no ロブ") so that was kinda weird, but it all worked out, I'm sure. I felt bad about marking that motorcycle, plus, oh great, on the first time we met I defaced private property.

Oh well.
