Just played 15 minutes of Hackey Sack Jack with Jesse, making it HSJJ. Money.

- - - -

We had been study/chatting in the teachers room, when, for no apparent reason, I pulled my hackey sack out of my pocket and tossed it to my other hand.

[blink] goes Jesse. "You play hackey sack?"

"Yeah, you?"

"Yeah (!) Let's go."

Jesse grabbed *his* hackey sack, which is a really quality footbag. Leather, with dense small pellets, and excellent for stalls. "Damn! This bag can't help but stall."

We played out kinda behind the building in a roped off area that we decided was just for our entertainment. "There's no room, otherwise." True enough to be true.

Jesse was able to do a tie stall three times. His hackey sackey style is mostly using feet; he can do inside kicks well, and his outside kicks got better through the 20 minutes that we played. (He hadn't played in a year before this.)

My style is mostly using my knees. I kept getting flubbed up when the sack would hit my lump of gloves in the front pocket of my hoodie, but I did generally well; I was glad and we both had way fun.
