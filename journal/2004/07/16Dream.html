I was talking to Mike Cutaia, my business distributor about a cassette tape by Jodi Victor. Mike quoted a funny part of the tape and asked me to play it so he could hear it again. Jodi started his speech making automobile sounds passing at high speed, implying the driver of the car was afraid to go beyond 55 miles per hour, using that as an excuse to not move his business forward.

I was impressed that Mike could quote the tape; he really had listened to it.
