8:04am JST Friday 28 May 2004

He asked me again to bring in the collected toilet paper roll projects. I was like, "oh; sorry for forgetting; I keep getting sidetracked," and I began to work on it. But it was almost too late.

We heard in the distance the high pitched whirling scrawling sound of the locusts coming through. I was like, oh, fuck! and grabbed as many of the larger designs that I could with one hand and put some more in my shirt like a kangaroo pouch.

We ran inside just as the screaming sound blasted by the house, and I watched the swarm squeal by like a huge undulating flying serpent.

I took some pictures, and some of them turned out really well, capturing individual insects in flight, but still giving an overview of the hoarde.
