Spinning Coaster

Aptly named, the spinning coaster is a variant of wild mouse traps that I've ridden before. Each car has two seats back to back, and the whole assembly spins according to weight distribution while going around the hairpin turns.

The line is usually short, and is worth about a five to ten minute wait. On one occassion, there was *no* line, and I rode it about 5 times in a row.
