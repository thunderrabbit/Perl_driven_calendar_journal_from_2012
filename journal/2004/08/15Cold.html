9:28am JST Sunday 15 August 2004

Well, I have to give Tokyo weather props for being cool (*), but I have to give it a growl for getting cold.

This morning, at 5am, through my window I heard a huge rush of an oncoming wall of rain. I'm not sure how to spell such a sound, but it was like, "rrrrrrRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRAR" as it ran toward my open window. It would have been cool to see, but it was dark, and we were sleeping, so Hitomi jumped up to close the window.

[ssshuup] (sound of window closing)

[RAR] (sound of rain pelting heavily)

"Wow." (that was a close call)

(*) cool in the "wow that was cool!" way, not in the "hey it's getting cold" way.

- - - -

But now it's *cold*!! Argh! I was loving loving loving the 35 C degree weather!
