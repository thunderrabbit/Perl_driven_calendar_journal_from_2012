5:47pm

Arrived in Nakameguro, headed out toward where I thought the shop was, and quickly determined that the simple map in my head was insufficient for me to find the bike shop. I knew there was a river involved, so I found the river and walked along it in the direction my brain indicated. After a while of walking, I decided that the shop must not be directly alongside the river. However, there were many apartments alongside the river, so I figured I would be able to smurf some free internet bandwidth for a moment. Walked along the river with my computer open, watching with iStumbler, a shareware (freeware?) thing that snoops for internet signals.

Found a few weak ones before I was able to find a strong one that didn't require a password. It didn't take really long; woulda been faster to start looking for an internet signal before I just set out. But anyway, I acquired a map and it was all good.

I walked a block to the street on which the bike shop was located and determined that I had walked well past the shop. Walked back toward the station; the shop was three traffic lights from the station, and traffic lights were pretty frequent.

Aha. After a while, bang I found the shop. I recognized the front of the shop from their picture online. It was smaller than I expected.

Also, it was closed.
