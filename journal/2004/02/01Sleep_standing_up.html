Today on the train, I fell asleep while standing in tadasana, the yoga standing pose I learned from Catherine. My sleep didn't last very long; I jerked awake when the train started moving and I hit the girl next to me on the arm. Oops. I felt bad at first, but I got over it with happy thoughts of how cool it is that I fell asleep standing up.
