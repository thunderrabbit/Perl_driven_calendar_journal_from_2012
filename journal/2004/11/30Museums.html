Went to Tokyo Photography Museum near Ebis with Daniel. The coolest thing was not aphotograph, but a sound wave visualizer that had little syrofoam beads in a horizontal plastic tube and a speaker box at one end. The speaker played tones and the styrofoam beads danced around in the low pressure zones of the rarefactions (sp) of the sound waves. Awesome cool! I got a video of it which I may put on here.
