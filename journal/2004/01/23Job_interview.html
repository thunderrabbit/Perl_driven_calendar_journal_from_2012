I saw a strange ad on the train today; one that had a book with really strange English wording on it: "Hello, Jane, what is the book that you are reading?" "Hello, John, I am reading the book of Arnold Whatsisname in order to write a paper of it." or some shit like that, and on the page on which it's written, is a picture of a young man and woman and then scribbling on the page, little doodles of graffitti including a disco light near the letters DISCO of "Discover" etc etc.

At the bottom of the page, was katakana that I slowly read:

マンツーマン英語

"ma n tsu ma n eigo"

"mantsu man eigo"

Man to man English!

So I wrote down the number and called them during janette's lesson. I have an interview with them on Tuesday 10 February at 10 am.
