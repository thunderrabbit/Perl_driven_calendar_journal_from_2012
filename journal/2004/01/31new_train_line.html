12:10am JST Sunday 1 February 2004

Holy cats.

My brain is kinda spinning from what I've seen here.

At this station (Tammachi), the Tokyu Toyoko line has been changed from going above ground level to being 4 stories below ground level. The change happened in one night. Or, actually, within four hours.

Now, granted, they didn't build this new station four stories below ground in four hours; this shit is so fly that they've probably been working on it since before I arrived in Japan.

But, yesterday, the trains ran on the tracks five stories up. Today they run right here.

The next station is still the old station with the old tracks with pigeon poo all over them. I got off at that station and followed the tracks back toward where the change occurred. The pictures didn't come out, but there are towers

Shit it's 2am; I'm tired as fuck. I was pissed at the guy in my train station for kicking me out; I just wanted a momento of this change. But all the old maps seem to be gone, and the old (unneeded as of today) posters are apparently too important to give away.
