I just got a Samsung 707SC, and I basically can't use it in conjunction with my Mac. Maybe it would have been a good idea to do more research before just signing a 2 year contract with them, but ah well. I've done dumber things.

Basically the phone is okay, but it has some silly quirks. I can save contact data to the phone or to the USIM card, but when I search for said data, I have to tell it which to search. It should just grab the data from both sources and display it in one list!

I can transfer addressbook data from (Address Book on) my Mac to the phone, but I can't save contact data from the phone to my computer. Why? Maybe there is some tweak I haven't tweaked yet, but so far, I can't figure it out.

I transferred a .swf file to my computer from the phone. It worked fine, and I was all excited to create a killa .swf for my phone, but I can't transfer .swfs to the phone. Barf. This is dumb.

So the phone is slim, and has a pretty high res camera, can surf the web (though I can't set my preferred home page), and read gmail, (but not download gmail's mobile app), but only 3 thumbs up out of 5.
