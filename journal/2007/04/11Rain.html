4:24pm JST Wednesday 11 April 2007 (day 13531)

Oooky blucky whiney rain today. It's stupidly chilly for April; I haven't seen the sun in days. But perhaps that's why we have the phrase about April showers and May flowers.

The rain apparently caught people off guard; I've seen a large percentage of Japanese without umbrellas. Normally if a drop falls here, all available umbrellas pop open.
