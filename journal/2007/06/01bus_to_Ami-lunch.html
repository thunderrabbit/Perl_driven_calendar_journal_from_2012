This is the first time I've taken the bus from Shibuya to Roppongi. Always taken the train before this. I generally like the trains better because I imagine them to be more likely on schedule, since they usually have their own uninterrupted paths on which to run. Plus there's a bit of a psychological factor: a bus can literally go anywhere; perhaps the driver will make a wrong turn and end up in France or something. A wrong turn on a train would take a more concerted effort by more than one person.
