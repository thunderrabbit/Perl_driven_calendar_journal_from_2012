In 五京, there are three genders: male, female and kimale (木メル (tree-male)). A baby can only be produced if a male and female have intercourse inside a kimale, where the zygote is left to grow. Therefore the females do not have to carry babies to term; the tree (木 (ki)) gives energy (気 (ki)) to the fetus during gestation.

Also, cars have five wheels and can roll in any direction. They're actually spheres instead of wheels as we think of them.
