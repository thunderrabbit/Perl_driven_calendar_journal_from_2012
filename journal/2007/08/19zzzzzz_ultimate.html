7:34am JST Monday 20 August 2007 (day 13662)

I just wrote a summary of the game on the Tokyo Ultimate forum.

Cited as the lowest turnout since "that time it snowed three years ago," game play featured three
skins on three shirts plus one sub, and then once Jason B arrived by bicycle from Shinagawa, 4 on
4. A decent breeze and consistent cloud cover kept the day just cool enough for everyone to play
several points in a row.

Near the end, we played a game to 5, during which Mike Ellis (our third Mike, if I understand
correctly) tried to retire, but we peer pressured him into finishing out the game, saying "just one
more point," a couple of times after the subsequent points continued to not finish the game.

Back to three on three, the game devolved into silliness, with all the right words being shouted,
but the disc going all the wrong directions to anyone who happened to be willing to run.

In attendance: Yoshio, Kerry, Wonderboy, Carl, Doug, Jeremy, American Mike, jei, and quietly cheerleading: Ami.
