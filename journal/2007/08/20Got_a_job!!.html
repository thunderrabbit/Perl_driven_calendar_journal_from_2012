I got a job!

I'm semi-shocked at how "easy" it was.

I emailed my resume to a few people I know.

Separately, on Friday I set up an interview with one of my ex-students (Akira).

I didn't remember who he was, but his card clearly said IT Recruiting.

He remembered me and set up an interview for today.

I thought I would just be meeting him, but he came in with another guy.

I remembered who Akira was.. He buys and sells companies for fun and profit.

I started talking to the one guy he brought in, and then three more guys came in.

My previous introduction was recounted in Japanese.

They asked a couple questions, talked for a while, and I caught almost nothing besides what I'll translate as , "do you have anything you can leave with us?"

ちょっとはずかしいんだけど、プリンタがないので、USBキーでresumeがある。

They were like, no problem, and produced a laptop onto which I copied my resume from my USB stick.

They all had a gander, talked some more, then, "do you want to work part time or full time?"

"Um.. part time, because there are other projects I'd like to work on."

"Okay, can you start September 1st?"

"Yes.

Wait that's a Saturday."

"Okay, September 3rd."

And that's how I got my first IT job in Japan.
