Okay, I'm running a white MacBook 1.83 GHz Intel Core Duo, and my battery said 86% and the machine just powered off. No sleep, no warning, just off.

I looked over and my power cord was unplugged from the wall. I hadn't noticed that detail. Ooops. But 86%!

I thought this would be a good time to do a battery reset: I unplugged the computer, removed the battery, pressed the power button for 5 seconds. And by 5 seconds I mean five times for about 10 to 20 seconds each.

Put the battery back in, (did *not* plug in the power), and turned it on. It powered on no problem, and the battery reported 85%.

Now the battery is reporting 80%, which sucks balls for sure, but it's proof (to me) that the battery reset did *something*.
