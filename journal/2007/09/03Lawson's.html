At Lawson's I saw Akami and Tsunashimsan for the first time in about 6 weeks. They were good and genki, though Akami no longer sports red hair! I considered changing her name, but couldn't really bear to change it.

I helped them stock new products and shuffle some old products off the shelves to make way for new items. We joked about me getting a job there. Even though at first she was like, "great! send me your resume!" the manager said it would be impossible to let me work for just a week. I was like, "not a week, just four days!"

I asked them how to redeem my hard earned (100 yen per) points, but the system they have doesn't allow me to just buy an 84 yen item for 84 points. I have to redeem for a coupon of either 150, 300, or 500 points, and then get no change back for the difference. Bah.

So Tsunashima-san was like, "here; I'll give you 84 yen until you get paid."

Awright.
