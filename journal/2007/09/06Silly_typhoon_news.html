I just stuck my head outside and opened the windows for a minute to let some fresh air come through the room.

Shinkansens are running again; they didn't seem to be running at 7:30 when Ami and I were out. I guess they are running late now; I think they normally finish by 1am.

I'm glad she was game, though she wore more rain protection than I, with her (my) hood pulled tightly around her head. She was so cute!
