7:40am JST Friday 21 September 2007 (day 13694)

Outside at a ski resort, with lots of friends around, watching a little sidewalk pond thing as translucent angel fish that looked more like piranha were coming up to the surface to get a chance to breathe air or bite the dogs that were interested in biting the fish.

The dogs were patiently waiting for the right fish to come up, be they waiting for angel fish or gold fish.

As I was looking down, I noted my friend the ski instructor had taken off his boots, leaving them in the way of the sidewalk. I threw some snow at him and he instantly whipped some back at me, harder than I had thrown it at him.

I watched a promotional video of the ski thing; we would be able to go backwards down the slope, which allowed us to do a flip at the end and come down forward toward the start again. I thought it was a great idea, but didn't know if I'd be able to go down backwards at the beginning.
