What is called "makeup" in US English is called "make" in Japanese. The girl standing next to me on the train to Shibuya had interesting eye makeup. Gold and green glitter eye shadow and dark mascara. I liked the gold and green. And the glitter. I was gazing at her eyes when she made eye contact with me. Instead of looking away, I held my gaze. And my poker face. I should have smiled. Ah well.
