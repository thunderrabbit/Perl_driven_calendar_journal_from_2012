There's an entry for Sakurashinmachi station on Wikipedia, but it's just a stub. Right about now, the station is undergoing construction, including new escalators and fire doors. Part of me would like to update the entry with pics from this construction.

The rest of me is busy with other things.

I saw some bars on the ceiling which look like tracks designed to hold a hanging curtain, but they were attached all shoddily. I asked a guy in Japanese what they were, and he answered in English, "pardon?" and then explained they are simply to hold the temporary plastic ceiling in place.
