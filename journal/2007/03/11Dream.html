7:03am JST Sunday 11 March 2007 (day 13500)

Jimmy da Magnet gave me 5000 yen to go get a drink; he told me to keep the change for my 13,500th day celebration. I quite happil went down to the neighborhood shop, which was tucked in an alleyway below the freeway by a big tree. I opened the refrigerator to get a drink and found a plate of fried eggs and a banana on the bottom that had been there a few days since I had forgotten them after cooking but not paying for them last week. I took the plate as well and went up to the counter, explained to the guy I had left the eggs by mistake.

He got all excited and called his friend over and I told him what happened, and they were like, "don't worry about paying for the eggs; we've had a betting pool on whether or not the eggs would be claimed and paid for. The betting pool more-than-covers the price of the eggs." and I laughed my ass off at the simplicity of the technique for not having to pay for forgotten-remembered food.
