I just realized something(s):

If God is infinite, then God must be the universe (or bigger than the universe)

Being in the "presence of God" is said to be the ultimate fulfilling feeling, from which we never want to leave

So God = good feeling, and God = infinite, so the only way to have bad feelings is to believe ( = create the (perceived?) reality of ) being away from God.

So ultimately, our desire as souls will be to merge with God, which would mean that we believe we are infinite, or, in other words, inseparable = the same as, everything/one/where/time, etc.

So, once we realize that all of these concepts of separation are false, and then realize that thinking about separation as a concept pulls us away from oneness, then we begin to realize the nature of reality, which is we are all one.

Anything I do to you, I do to myself, though the perception may be otherwise, because I perceive you as not being me.

Religions based on doing good things are specialized cases of this, all trying to be more like God, = remember that we are already one, = unlearn that we are separate.

And God-ness is just patiently and gently waiting.
