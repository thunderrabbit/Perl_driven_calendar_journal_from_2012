11:24am JST Saturday 5 May 2007 (day 13555)

At Jonathon's I told Ami that I have always really thought she was beautiful and often want to kiss her and stuff. She said she was a bit embarrassed, but I was like, "I'm *more* embarrassed!" and we took a picture of me with a dot of ketchup on each cheek to represent my embarrassment.

Finished eating, but I wasn't finished talking, though I was getting a bit tired. I suggested we walk to the nearby little river, which we did. I was happily surprised that I found it so easily, coming toward it from a new direction, against what part of me was thinking should be the fastest direction, but toward what the rest of me thought. Weird to explain, but the logic used a bit of a corollary to Fred's Law: "parallel streets in Japan are not parallel" We walked hand in hand a lot after that, including along the small river, and walking to Kosugi from Motosumiyoshi, where we had been semi-snuggly side by side in chairs while watching the trains.

I told her I was thinking about going to a hotel, and she agreed it would be nice, but pointed out that it's really an important topic, so we should wait.

I agreed. Happily agreed.

At the Nambu line gates, we planned our next meeting: 10am (24 hours from now) at Futakotamagawa. Hugged her for a decent amount of time. "I really had fun last night and today." "Me too."
