6:24am CDT Friday 20 July 2007 (day 13632)

Apparently, it's been raining every day for about a month, which actually is pretty unusual for this time of year in Houston. I just got woken up by a rather strikingly loud thunder strike, and.. man, I wish Ami were here to see this. It's not the hardest rain I've ever seen, but *this* is rain.

This normal summer storm (only abnormal in the recent frequency, not intensity) is raining harder and producing more lightning than *any* storm I've seen in 4.25 years in Tokyo. This is why I have no respect for what Japanese call "rain" in Tokyo as I walk around without my umbrella, assuring them, "no, actually, it's *not* raining."
