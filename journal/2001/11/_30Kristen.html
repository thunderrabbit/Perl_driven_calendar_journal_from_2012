My intention is not to write to you through this medium. It's ass-backwards and such. But I am aware that you are able to read it. It's my intention to write whatever I would have written even if you were not. Like this:

I gave someone access to this secret journal recently. I'm trusting that all the details are kept quite ....... private. It's not that I am ashamed of what I love in sex, but that I don't just go telling lots of people about it.

I secretly hoped that Kristen would be all, "oh my god that sounds fucking fantastic," and ask me if we could have such wild wonderful sex as some of my adventures describe. I planned to respond, "only if we can make it a full evening and overnight lovemaking experience."

I get horny thinking about that. But if I look at it from a grander scale, I remember that I intend to *not* get involved sexually with anyone during my alone time. To get into a relationship with someone is totally out of the question. To get involved sexually should probably be totally out of the question, but dammit I fucking miss it a lot. But that's not it quite so much as I just miss it some, as I've been filling my time with creative artistic outlet type stuff. Painting, learning languages, building my marble track.

Another part of me knows that I can only partner with someone who is such an incredibly close friend, that we've been friends for years, through moves, through anger, through months without talking, and then instantly reconnecting with no hurt feelings and lots of stories. I have that with Sally and with Kristen.

Truth is that I am leaning toward Sally because of the incredible sex that I know she is capable of. I don't think Kristen is a prude, but I just don't have anything to work with there. One day I want to bring sex into our friendship (do the semantics even support that possibility?) friendship including sex? I think it can. I don't know. I'm not ready yet though. I trust that one day I will know when I'm ready, and that I'll know what I'm ready for.

Kristen, do you know what I just realized? Your ability to read these innermost secrets of my soul remind me of a scene from a book called The Blue Adept, I think. In it, robots are at least as smart and capable as humans. One cannot tell them apart easily. A human found out that his friend was a robot, so he asked if he could "download her information" or something. She finally agreed, and later the human character realized this action amounted to a rape. He had violated her innermost thoughts.

I see that here, not as a rape; I am consenting. But truth is, I am allowing you to know my innermost thoughts, trusting that you will receive them as a blessing and with love. In return, I would like to know more of your mind.

I love you. Really.
