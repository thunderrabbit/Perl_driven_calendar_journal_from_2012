October 2001

I'm at work. I'm desperately trying to get anything done so I can feel like I've done a good job. I'm spinning my proverbial wheels.

Listening to Beastie Boys to keep me company. The silence is difficult to bear. I just turned them off. Just me and my clickity clackity fingers.

on my way to writing this entry, I got a message from Forest C Riggs. How amazing. I've not heard from him literally in *years* since he said he wished to break off all contact. Ironically, I was just at the point then that I was willing to be friends with him via snail mail.

I'm sad. I am about to go home. The house is so big and Wende so far away up 3 flights of stairs. I've lost my keys, but have cobbled together enough to get my through a few days, but I can't lock up my bike. I have misplaced my extra copies of my bike lock keys.

I am going to go home now - ride my bike all the way back. Is it all the lonely way back, or am I just riding my bike back? Enjoying the cool night air, or is it fucking freezing? It's all in how I look at it.

I haven't written that I got a 12% raise today. 15% last year, 12% this year. Not too bad. Especially considering that the economy seems shallow.

Okay bye.
