4pm Saturday 29 September 2001

After ultimate, I got lost (and really pissed) on the way to meet with Kim Sawyer. I quickly felt better once I got there; he had no judgement and said that the highways do that to him as well. I got 59 south mixed up with 610 west loop.

We immediately started talking about whatever I wanted to talk about which was relationship with Wende and he pointed out that it sounds like I'm back to where I was with her some weeks ago - on schedule to be married, but not being ready in my heart for such a manuever.

True true true fucking dammit I'm glad you pointed that out. I am back in relationship with Wende because my friends want me to be back (because change makes them uncomfortable as well) and because it feels easier to be back (even if it's not better)

Kim pointed out that love is not enough to have a relationship. That I can love Wende and love Sally just as much, but there can be certain similarities - in metabolism, place in life, goals, etc that make some relationships more compatible than others.

He is not suggesting any decision for me, but just to be sure that I really think about these things - that I don't just go back to relationship with Wende because it is more comfortable. Just because the personal work is hard, doesn't mean that I shouldn't do it.

So, dammit, here I am again, looking for some external force to break us up or give us the mathematically provably correct answer. I am not clear that I'll find that answer anytime soon; it sounds like I need to be alone longer.

My Higher Self said, "a year is a good start" on how long to be alone. Wow. But, ya know - a year isn't really all that long. One cycle of the seasons. One new directory in my website. One set of rallies. One year.

So how do I do this and feel good about the decision? Go into my own feelings; pray about it. Intend for the right path to be shown and help me be guided down that path, no matter how hard or difficult it seems to take.
