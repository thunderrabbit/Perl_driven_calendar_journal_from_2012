10:02am Thursday 20 September 2001

As I finished my previous entry, Wende walked in. I knew that I could wait no longer.

Lots of tears, one big hug, told her how scared I was. Told her that I couldn't marry her and that I didn't know what to do. Told her what Kristen said. Told her I was thinking about Sally. Did not tell her that I have had sex with Sally.

Wende said that she has been invited to live with Maggie.

I wanted her to do that, and to take care of myself, amidst a lot of fear, I asked her if she would live with Maggie for a while. I cried cried cried. She said she would, and we agreed that one week would be a good timeframe.

I told her that I didn't want this, but I had to do it. I had to do it to take care of myself.

I went to Doug Sorensen and told him the situation, and he said I was doing good work, etc, and that I was on The Path and taking care of myself in an adult way.

Upon my query, he said that it is possible for me to take care of myself even if Wende is living with me. That I don't have to not live with her, but I can still take care of myself even if she is with me.

One step at a time, I move on.
