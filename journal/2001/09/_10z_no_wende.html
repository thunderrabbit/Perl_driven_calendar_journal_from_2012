9:25pm CDT Monday 10 September 2001

I got home from Spanish tonight, and Wende wasn't home. She wasn't at Spanish either, but I kinda expected that. I almost kinda expected her to not be home.

She wrote me a note indicating that she feels sad and that she couldn't bear to spend the night here with me because that would give an illusion of normalcy that she knows isn't there. She has invited me to attend the Thursday therapy session with her and Carmella, and asked me to invite Doug if I want.

I'm going to go to sleep and cry now. Right now, right *now* there are no tears. No emotions. All blocked. Anger is on top of that holding them all down. Fear actually.

I'm so fucking scared and sad and don't know what to do.

Molly said that I seem to be putting too much stock in YRUU.

First, there were basically two types of responses to my letter that I wrote to all those peeps:

1) You can have YRUU and Wende; they are not mutually exclusive.

2) In order for the relationship to work, you have to *love* Wende and choose Wende on a daily basis.

Molly suggested that I tend to run away from things when they get hard. I can see that I ran away from PAG (the group I worked in at Telecheck for a while, which sucked, so I quit), and I have run away from other things I guess. All relationships?

So if in fact I am running away from each relationship that gets to the point of difficult choices, then it seems I need to heal something in me. Should I be in a relationship while I'm doing that? If I'm in a relationship while healing a relationship wound, then what does that do to the relationship?

I haven't called any of my girlfriends lately, nor even during this crisis, although I did email it also to Sally and Madeline. Sally wrote me back. Her answer was in genre 2 above. So, anyway, I haven't called any of my girlfriends. I'm going to call my dad now.

06:09am Tuesday 11 September 2001

Called my dad and wasted no time.

"Good evening," he answered the phone.

"Hi Dad."

"HI Rob, how's it going?"

"Crappy."

I blurted the last couple of days and that Wende wasn't there and just started to cry with, "and I don't know what to do."

Huge huge gasping gaping sobs and tears and snot all pouring out of my face, body all crunched up squeezing out each cry until gasp of air and start all over. Dad just kept soothing me on, "good job, yeah, let it all out," over and over

Major issues came to light from this 5 year old part of me:

1) I believed that no one loves me.

2) I believed that I didn't have any friends.

3) I believed I didn't know what to do.

Each time I stopped crying, Dad asked something like, where are you now? or what else? or something and then I was all, "" then cry cry cry cry cry again.

Finally when I was all cried out for the night, Dad said, "okay, breathe in golden light to fill in where the sadness and anger had been." I spent about 5 minutes doing this until I was relaxed and tranquil.

Then he encouraged me to say, "I forgive myself for judging myself as alone." and "I forgive myself for judging myself as unloved."

Then, "for the truth is, I am surrounded with friend. I am my own friend," and "for the truth is, I am very lovable and very loved."

I repeated those a few times, and wow I must say it felt awkward and uncomfortable for me to say them. Some shame in there trying to keep me from saying them.

I was able to own the phrases, and I even added one, "I forgive myself for judging myself as not smart. For the truth is I am brilliant."

"Yes." he encouraged. "Say that one again."

"I forgive myself for judging myself as not smart. for the truth is I am brilliant."

After a few moments he asked, "okay, from this space, which is more important? Wende or YRUU?"

"Me. I am most important." was the first answer that came to my head, but I didn't say it. I thought there was a right answer between the two choices he gave. I actually was looking for the right answer.

"Understand that there is no wrong answer," or something like that.

"The first answer that came to my head is that I'm most important."

"Good; you are most important. Thank you. Great."

Wow. I'm glad to have gotten such support for my truth.

Choosing Wende or YRUU as more important still did not result in a response. Just me. I'm most important.

Dad summarized the phone call, from the points of sadness above to the healing of judgements to the simple fact that I am most important.

I thanked him and said I'm going straight to bed now. I did.

I checked the time and the conversation had lasted 50 minutes.
