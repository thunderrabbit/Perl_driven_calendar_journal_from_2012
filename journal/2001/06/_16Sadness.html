11:37am CDT Saturday 16 June 2001

I touched the bottomless pit of sadness this morning. Got home from the lock in and got in the shower with Wende, who said I could, "only to get clean [and not do anything sexual]."

I got in and felt really sad and watched as she shaved and wished I could just sit on the floor and watch her shave, and not do anything else.

She finally got out of the shower and I still felt really sad and I said, "Doug Sorensen thinks I might be going through withdrawal."

"What's that?"

"It means the same thing as for drugs, but I don't really know what it means."

I described a sad thing or two: "I'm afraid that I might have been inappropriate with Heidi Hardt last night. At the lock in we played a game where we pass around questions that are like, "who here is most likely to _________" or "who would you share your deepest secrets with?" or "Who here is your second persona?" or "Who would you want to be for 24 hours?" and each player writes the name of someone who they thought fit that thing.

"When the question was "Who would be your dream date?" Heidi was said to me, "put me as your answer." and I did, using a previously written copy of her name that had hearts written around it. I told her that I used the copy with hearts. I was flirting with her, secretly wondering if, if everything else were different and she was not my youth, and she were way older, if we could go out."

I got out of the shower and cried a little in my towel. Then I lay on the bed on my side and cried a ton.

Huge gaping chasm of infinite black sadness not enveloping me, but just feeding me sadness. So sad. So crying. It got worse when I thought that maybe I had been inappropriate as an advisor. Oh god I don't want to be inappropriate as an advisor.

Wende came and sat beside me; didn't touch me or speak, just listened.

"I don't want to be a bad youth advisor." Crying crying crying "Sally was so supportive of me this weekend, saying that I'm on the right track and that I can do this and that she can imagine it's hard work and that she knows I can do it." Crying crying crying.

Crying crying crying, snot bubbles from my nose and I didn't even care. Still on my side.

"Something has changed since I asked you to marry me. I know that we were having sex before that, or I wouldn't have asked you. I know that I haven't cheated on you since Amanda and therefore we were having sex after that and before I asked you, but something has changed and I don't know what it is. I don't know what to do about it.

"I'm afraid that I have done something wrong and that fits in with my wanting to do things right and to be good; I don't want to do things wrong and I'm afraid that I've done something wrong and I don't know what it is."

I giggled slightly at the irony, "whatever it was, I take it back."

I wondered if the act of crying (so sad that all the air is being pushed out of the lungs) is physically pushing out bad energy and deep breaths bring in good energy - the physical animal's way of blowing out the negative energy and bring in good. Interesting theory.

I was done crying that this point and washed my face and asked Wende if she would pick at my shoulder. She said she would compromise and scratch on my back if I lay down. I didn't really want that; I wanted her to pick at my shoulder, but I accepted and lay down. She scritched my back until I was relaxed and she asked if I could fall asleep. I could and I did. She read beside me as I slept. I wouldn't let her touch my hand or anything.
