10:06am Saturday 2 June 2001

I went to the first hour of SLAA meeting, starting at 9am. 26 people were there at 9am and 50-few people were there when I left at 10. Bunch of older guys, mostly older than me, and a couple of guys about my age, but they seem quieter than the old guys, like me. I didn't say anything. If I was called upon and actually asked to speak I would have told them that I wasn't particularly happy about being there. Pretty pissed, and have been just fine recovering by going to my therapist.

But they didn't call on me, and I didn't say a single word. Not even "HI ____" to whoever was speaking.

I wasn't so pissed by the time I left; I even opened my eyes for the last 30 minutes. My eyes were closed for the first half or so, except to peek at most of the late people as they came in, wondering if I would see either Jim or Ben or Kim, three of my original warrior friends.

They weren't there, and I had no remorse when I left at 10, even though the meeting was still pretty much in full effect.
