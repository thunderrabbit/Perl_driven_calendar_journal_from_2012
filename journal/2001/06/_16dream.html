11:28am CDT Saturday 16 June 2001

Dream about an older man possibly targeting me as sexual prey. I was talking about it to an old man, and I was my current age (30 some odd) and he hadn't actually done anything, but the guy I was talking to said he might just be thinking about it.

I told the guy I was talking to that if the one guy does anything I will immediately terminate my relationship with him, and that will be the end of it.

Then the guy I was talking to turned a corner and touched the back of my head in a propping me up kind of way. It didn't feel sexual, but was kinda weird because we had just been talking about the possibility of someone else trying that.

Further away in my dream, I was with my youth group and feeling the bottomless pit of sadness. I walked away from the group to cry cry cry. I could hear them asking where I was, and I didn't say anything, but I raise my hand in a thumbs up motion so that in case they saw me, they would know I was okay.

I sat on the side of the street and kinda cried, and then a foreigner found a street sign in the street and marveled that we could have street signs in the street and not properly label the streets; how could a new person get around? This was exactly the street he needed to find when he was first here.

He took the sign in order to put it back where it goes. The correct street wasn't too far away, and I wondered how they would correctly verify that was it. I figured there would be some other identical signs on the same street at different blocks. The street was only two blocks long though, and there was a large building in the middle of it. Or more specifically, there was a large building, and the street extended one block on either side of the building.
