I just finished my 2010 taxes here in Japan. It's the first time I've filed taxes here in 4 years. The paperwork for the previous 3 years has been scattered via neglect because I didn't make a lotta money and figured it wasn't worth the trouble.

Today, thanks to filing my 2010 taxes, I will get all of the tax I paid in 2010 back in my bank account. That's cold hard notes of cash to myself. 19,200 yen worth, to be exact.

To be in self-perceived good standing with Japanese tax system, I gotta frackin' harass my old employers to give me copies of my W2 equivalents for the previous years.

Note to self: do yer taxes each year. And keep all your medical receipts. You'll probably get a little spring bonus of cash back.
