I get a lot of spam. Well, not a lot lot lot, but maybe 50 per day. (haven't tested this recently). Some spam gets through to my inbox, usually by having a bunch of random words in it.

Just thinking that it would be way more effective for spambots to read the top page or two of the domain of the intended recipient (or in the case of hotmail, yahoo or other portal/web-based addresses, read the pages of text from where the address was gleaned), quote parts of the text from that scan, and send it along with the spam URL to the email address. That way the spam will likely be natural language, the recipient will likely be attracted to things relating to the recipient's interest, etc.
