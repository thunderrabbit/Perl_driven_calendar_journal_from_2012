We should all be enjoying days instead of bemoaning years.

  1. Every day is original and unique

  2. Counting days is far more interesting that counting years

  3. Celebrations happen in various seasons (*)

  4. Party sizes vary according to the number of zeros in the age-count

  5. Seem more humble by celebrating only every 500 days

  6. Know who really cares (!)

(*) especially good for people whose birthdays are eclipsed by national holidays

(!) takes more effort to remember a celebration with inconsistent dates
