Met a cool student named Shinpei today who is like the perfect match for me. He was like, "don't tell me good job unless I'm really doing well." (or at least that's basically what he meant.) He locks on to adjustments I give just as well as little Riho the 14 year old, even though he's 30, totally blowing away the curve of younger students better able to retain language.

We can have a real conversation, and even though I interrupt him with corrections, often restructuring his entire sentence, we get right back on the topic and it's great.

He's just started a new job with a clothing store of some sort. In 3 months his bosses expect him to be in charge of opening markets overseas.
