Around 6am, there was a big boom that sounded exactly like the sunrise/sunset cannon we heard in Jerusalem during Ramadan. It woke me up, but I recognized the sound, so I wasn't too scared. Then I heard several more in the distance, from various directions, so I knew either (1) they were loud but harmless cannons for which nothing should be done, or (0) it was a huge attack on the city about which nothing could be done.

They continued at various locations and intervals, and I had a dream snippet for a close one:

a bomb was thrown near me and accidentally set part of my sleeping bag on fire.

It's overcast and cold this morning.
