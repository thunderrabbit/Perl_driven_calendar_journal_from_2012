1:39pm JST Monday 24 April 2006 (day 13179)

It's been a couple of days, so I don't think I'll try to do justice to writing a full entry about it.

Suffice that the ride was long, and had a couple of great downhill zooms, and I was really happy to see Richard again, and super thankful to be treated to dinner, and to have a warm bed for sleeping purposes.

I took pictures of different tunnels; I'll use those as touchpoints for a story.


boat on building

I was surprised to see this boat on top of the building, even though I had seen an billboard for it a couple miles before.

I saw it and was like, "that's worth a picture!"
good night's sleep

I rode a realllly long time after the amazing gift of hamburgers from Mamirun and friends. At one point I stopped at a pedestrian overpass, and sat on the steps and at one of the cheese burgers. I literally fell asleep while eating it!

Woke up, finished eating, continued riding; I wanted to get to highway 163. Around 2:30am (April 22nd) I stopped to sleep here. I got up early; this picture was taken at 5:24am on the 22nd!

long (1.1km) Kiyotaki Daiichi tunnel

This tunnel was *amazing* for me on bicycle. Super long, super loud, *and* (if I remember correctly), I almost ate it when the sidewalk made a dogleg in the middle of the tunnel!! I was like, "what's that up ahead?" as some abberation in the sidewalk quickly sped toward me...

OH SHIT! THAT'S A RAILING!!!

And I slammed on my breaks (which didn't work for crap), and slammed down my foot to drag and stop.. Whew!!!!!!
