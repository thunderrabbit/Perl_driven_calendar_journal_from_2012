model airplane wings being attached with rubber bands; the guy was showing me how to do it, thought he was not able to do it himself, but he knew the theory. Rub the rubber band on the finger a bit to get some oil on it, twist it around the finger like this, and then stretch and rotate and hook it on the other peg.

I tried the technique, but was also having some trouble; I forgot to lubricate the rubber band and it broke; I dropped the wings, which got a bit scuffed. My guy tsked a little bit, but knew it couldn't be helped, plus they would get a lot more damage when actually being used.

He asked me what had happened. When I told him the rubber band broke, he said I should ask for a replacement one from the store, where we happened to be arriving. I hadn't thought of that.
