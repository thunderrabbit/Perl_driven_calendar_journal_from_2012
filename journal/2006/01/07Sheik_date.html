9:00pm SGT Saturday 07 January 2006

Tonight was Carl, Dan and my date with Carla, Yuki and Rei across the hall. I dressed in my white gown from Egypt and red/white checked head thing from Jordan. I became Mr Sheik, and Dan and Carl were my body guards in business suits and sunglasses. I put Francois looking out my front pocket (on the left at chest level) to complete the silly outfits.

We tried walking side by side with them flanking me as we walked through the ship, but the corridors were too narrow, so we resorted to single file.

Relatively little response as we walked around the 7th deck of the ship, but Maruan recognized it as a gag of unparalleled genius and took some pictures of us.

Our arrival in Yacht Club was much more appreciated; several people took pictures, and our three dates were like, "oh my god!" etc. For super added hilarity Forward Body Guard (Dan) served Mr Sheik's food and carried it to the table.

The Forward Body Guard tasted Mr Sheik's food and water to make sure it wasn't poisoned. We then dropped the pretense but joked like, "what if we had forced everyone to clear this side of the room before Mr Sheik came in??.." etc.. hilarious!!

During dinner, we asked the girls what they thought of the letters and which ones were allocated to whom and who they thought wrote which ones. They guessed Carl for the eloquent one, then me for the 'normal' one (perhaps Carla recognized my handwriting), but that left Dan for the misspelled one in crayon.

It seems they never decided which ones were for which of them.

We never gave them a straight answer, only "one of us likes one of you; one of us likes two of you; and one of us likes all three of you lovely ladies.." the last spoken like The Ladie's Man.

After dinner walk around to the front deck, we gazed at the ships on the horizon and joked that they were all pirate ships coming to get us. The wind was brisk; Yuki in short sleeves stayed huddled behind me; I enjoyed the bit of contact and warmth, because it began to sprinkle big drops of rain.

Down the front secret entrance and in pairs, with Front Body Guard in front and Back Body Guard in back, we paraded through free space and into Hemmingway, where I was called habibi by one of the arabic speaking bartenders. I don't remember his name, but he was on 49th voyage.

We joked that Mr Sheik should pay for everything, obviously being incredibly rich, and then joked about an incredibly poor sheik, whose oil field has dried up, and has no palace, no cars, no food... as I am still on my quest to spend 0 dollars on the ship.

(( although it must be noted that Hitomi has blessed me with $50 for laundry (and chocolate) on the ship; she wants to make sure I have clean clothes available. I'm not sure how I will go about buying laundry tokens and chocolate, but not putting it on my card. ))

They bought four ice creams and two brownies. I had a bit, not feeling comfortable in taking much of what I didn't pay for.
