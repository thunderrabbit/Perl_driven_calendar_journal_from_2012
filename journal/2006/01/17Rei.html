Rei and I had our second date tonight; we totally skipped going to Peter's Band's performance, and stayed out watching the stars on front deck from 8:35 to 10:10ish pm when they closed the deck.

During that time we calculated that she is 10487 days old, and therefore her 10500th day can be celebrated on 30 January! Wow!

It took a bit longer for us to calculate my birthday starting with 27 Oct 2005 = 13000 days, but we got it. I was truly impressed. We started by calculating the number of days in a leap year cycle, and then rounding that up to 1500, and then doubling that and subtracting 3000 day chunks from my days old and calling them 8 year chunks. Got down to 1000 days after doing four 8-year chunks, and then adding to 1000 the number of days we had overestimated by saying 1500 = 4 years. 1312 days actually, and that's *almost* another 4 year chunk, so we subtracted 1312 from 1461 and got 149, which is the number of days we need to add to 27 Oct 2005 to get to my next 8 year chunk, which happens to be my next birthday.
