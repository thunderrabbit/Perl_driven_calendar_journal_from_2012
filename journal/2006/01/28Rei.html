12:31am SAST Sunday 29 January 2006

Hung out with Rei tonight: we ate down in Topaz (I just had the appetizer and dessert) and then went to Victor's presentation. He's a self-taught photo journalist who grew up within Apartheid. His story amazed us all, especially to see how warm and loving he is toward life and everyone now. He has a lot he could be bitter about, but instead he chooses to see how much he has, after having everything ripped away from him before.

We went and walked around on top deck, looking at stars.
