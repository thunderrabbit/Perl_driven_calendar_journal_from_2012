1:00pm SGT Saturday 07 January 2006

The Fruits Party was today; all sorts of fruits were available buffet style for 1000 yen per person. The staff and volunteer staff helped greet people in a long-ass queue into Topaz Dining, and then the hula team performed hula up in front of the room. It seemed to go quite well, and everyone had fun.

After our performance, Peter's Band performed; they were quite genki and amazing with their drum stylin's. Peter's Japanese is quite good; I'm impressed.

For the actual fruit eating part, I enjoyed a lot of dragon eyes, mango, canteloupe, jack fruit, and dorian. The dorian was good only if I didn't sniff it.
