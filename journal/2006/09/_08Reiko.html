Reiko had asked to talk with me after work last night, and I was happy to do so. She basically asked me to tell her all of my problems, so I told her about my relatioship history, including girlfriends and overlapping girlfriends, and abortion, a miscarriage, and a slow process of learning appropriate behavior in relationships.

She described her gift of having been loved by both parents, and that her father always told her that he loved her (and sister, I presume), and though he died 2 years ago, she can still hear his voice in her heart, "Reiko, I love you."

I told her I wanted to be loved, and I wanted to be loved by Mommy, therefore I need to be my own Mommy to that little boy inside me.

She asked if I had ever "deeply loved" someone, so I described my definition of love (especially given she said she didn't know what it meant) before saying I had deeply loved Wende, and janette.

And by my definition, I love most of my students; I work hard to give them exactly what they need and want in their English learning process.

Reiko described her desire to use her gift of understanding people's problems, and said she can tell what problems all the customers have (they're not "students" to her), and she wants to help "all" of them.

I thought back to Richard Unger telling the woman at Diane's palm reading party "your job is to learn how to turn off that ability," and I was saying that to Reiko, but said "you need to remember you are Reiko. Your emotions are your own. Your job is to, not turn off the ability, but be able to see their emotions and then let them go,"

She was like, "I want to help all of them"

Woah.. that's the point right there. You can't help all of them. Not everyone wants to be helped. If someone comes to you, asking for help, then you may have an opportunity to help. But, you literally cannot help everyone...

She said "I got it" before I continued on my tirade.
