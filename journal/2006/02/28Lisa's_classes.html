Lisa's classes were not the problem I thought they would be. I could even imagine teaching them again, and would actually prefer one of her classes over one of mine. (WTF is that about?)

One of her classes featured three students, who arrived on time, 3 minutes late, and 6 minutes late, respectively. I told them I felt bad for Lisa because it's so hard to have to start over a class for each new student who comes in. I asked them "do you like Lisa?" and they were like, "yes" so I suggested they come on time to class tomorrow. They all seemed interested in doing that. I wonder if she'll notice.
