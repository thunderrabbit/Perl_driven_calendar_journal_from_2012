Watched _The Corporation_, a 3 hour documentary that talked about the history of corporations, how corporations have the rights of people but not the souls of people, how corporations are legally bound to watch their bottom lines, and how people have been able to fight corporations into doing something closer to the right thing.

It talked about sustainability, and suggested that basically no corporations are sustainable. They are plundering the earth of its natural resources: minerals, plants, animals (including humans), all in search of the might profit.

I wonder how sustainable my life is.
