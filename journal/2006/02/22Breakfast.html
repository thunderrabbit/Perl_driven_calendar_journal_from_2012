I have eaten breakfast in Yacht Club basically every day of the voyage. E In general, I have been getting way more sleep than I did on the 49th.

Yacht Club has the same breakfast buffet each day: granola cheese slices, salami slices, various breads (raisin bread, raisin cinnomin twists, sometimes croissants, sometimes corn flour sugar rolls) jelly, butter, orange juice, yogurt.

I usually get granola, a couple rolls, strawberry jelly, two or three (small) glasses of orange juice, and yogurt. Mix the jelly with the plain yogurt, mix that with the granola, chow.
