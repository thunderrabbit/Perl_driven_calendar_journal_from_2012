In line to ride a roller coaster, but e knew the ride operators, so we were allowed to climb on the structure of the ride a little bit while we were waiting for the train. I told my friend where she could safely stand out of the way, and explained what type of injury she could expect if she got hit by the train.

We waited up near the top of a slope, but when the train came back around, it went past us to the next section to pick up passengers. We marched down the track a bit sheepishly and piled ourselves into the train with a bit of discussion about who should get to sit where. I had immediately chosen the back left seat, and there was no question about whether I'd be allowed to sit there. Two more seats were available in the back.

As we started to ride, the train didn't go fast enough so we weren't going to make it over a loop, and a guy was showing us a model of the track and where the loop was arranged. The track model was weird; the loop was a ring and I couldn't see how the train could get either into our out of the loop.

I noticed a part where the track flattened; it was usually an inverted V shape like a ^ so like a monorail, but then it flattened out to a __ so perhaps the train could slide horizontally onto the track.
