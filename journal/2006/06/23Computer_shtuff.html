11:10am JST Friday 23 June 2006

Jesse and I are going to Oimachi to hopefully find some cool, inexpensive computer stuff ... for nerds. (us)

I want a USB US style keyboard.

3:02pm

Ended up getting a US style PS/2 keyboard, which I know won't fit into my/Hitomi's computer without an adapter, but my gut said buy it. 500 yen. And then Jesse pointed out that the keyboard features Mac-style command key and stuff, and says Mac compatible on the back. Nice! I didn't consciously notice that before I bought it.

Also of note, the guy inadvertantly gave me the PS/2 cable that I put on the counter with the keyboard. Like before I said I was going to buy the keyboard, he gave me the cable, apparently not knowing it had been theirs. I accepted it, which is one (conscious) reason I bought the keyboard.

They had an actual Mac USB keyboard for 1200 yen, but it was Japanese style layout, which I've already got with Hitomi's computer, so I chose not to buy it.

Jesse thinks I won't be able to find an adapter for less than 1000 yen; I neither agree nor disagree.
