8:53am JST Thursday 22 June 2006

A reporter made a young boy famous after he (the boy) survived a rough accident, and then went on to be a notable wind surfer, and featured in footage from a helicopter during a pretty decent sized hurricane along the coast.

The boy was given $250, which he donated to charity, and a rock garden was named in his honor. The rock garden was more the size of a rock flowerbed, but the rocks were still nice. Big naturally shaped pieces of basalt.

Especially if basalt is rough black "lava" rock.
