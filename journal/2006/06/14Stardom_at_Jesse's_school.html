5:13pm JST Wednesday 14 June 2006

Just got back from hanging out with Jesse, starting at the high school where he teaches thrice a week. He had told his students about me and my accident; they all wrote get well notes which he brought me while I was in the hospital.

Because he had told them all about me, I was pretty much like a star for a day in those classes, with a lot of the girls squealing and wanting to take pictures with me (or both of us) and stuff. Two of them asked me if I had a girlfriend.

It was definitely fun for a day, and I would definitely do it again, but I can't imagine doing it all the time.
