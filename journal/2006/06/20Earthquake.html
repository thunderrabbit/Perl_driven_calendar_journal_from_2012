While writing my dream, we had a good shakey shakey earthquakey! This building shakes "all the time" due to heavy traffic on the road and elevated highway right out front. Sometimes it feels like an earthquake, but then we get an actual earthquake that puts all questions to rest. That was an earthquake.

The shake is bigger, more dramatic, and accompanied by a creak in the walls somewhere as the building flexes to accomodate the wave.

I felt the shaking subside within about 5 seconds, and half hoped for a reshake, but nothing else has yet occurred (7:05am)
