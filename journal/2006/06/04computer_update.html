oops

Little glow of burning out a component in the adapter.

Shit.

Worked no problem.

(I was quite surprised the adapter worked after that little glowout.)

Damn.

She offered to take care of that (big blessing!) and still loaned me her computer, and I loaned her mine with broken screen and broken HD. What a great trade!
