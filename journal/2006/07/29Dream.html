7:54am JST Saturday 29 July 2006

In a YRUUer-filled guest house type place, and it was my first night there. I needed to get settled and the next morning get some food. I found some bananas in the kitchen, and took 1 that looked just slightly freckled, and then a saw an even larger banana collection by the sink with even more freckles, so I took one of those and replaced the first one.

I wanted some cereal and found a seemingly abandoned pot of milk and cereal, still cold, and a bit crunchy. Katie Lucach was on the other side of the screen in the kitchen and I asked her if it was her cereal. She said, "no; it's yours," and I wondered if she had the authority to give it to me, or just the wisdom of living here for a while to know it would be wasted if I didn't eat it.
