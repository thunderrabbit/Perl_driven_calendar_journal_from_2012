7:34am JST Sunday 9 July 2006

Back in Jerusalem, walking near where a road was being repaved against the Palestinian's wishes, over an area they considered sacred. I could feel the weight of stares on me as I walked through the affluent Israeli neighborhood and into the Palestinian neighborhood, with both sides wondering which side I was on.

As I walked along, I realized I shoulda brought my camera; this even would be remembered for years and it would be nice to have pictures from when it happened.

As I walked along the newly paved road, I looked up at the wall which was a chalk board from history class, with a writing assignmeent quickly scrawled, asking us to describe the historical background leading to this event and its likely affect toward the future.

I wished I had been listening when the assignment had been written, as I wasn't quite able to make out all the words, but I had a general idea of what it was. I figured I was in a pretty good place, though; I was the only student in the class who had been present when the road was being repaved.
