9:36am JST Saturday 14 October 2006 (day 13352)

A bit peeved that I missed the train to Shibuya by like 30 seconds. It was "random" though, I didn't consciously know what time the train was scheduled to leave; I hadn't looked it up before arriving at the station.

On the platform, I noticed the clumps of people standing where the doors would soon arrive on the upcoming train. Noticed 1 person with me, though all other clumps that I could see had 3 or 4 people. Interesting. I assume it's a form of racism, which I've encountered many times in Japan.

As the train arrived, the crowds inside reminded me I could optimize my exit strategy in Shibuya by getting on at the next door, then coming over to my door at the next station and get on last; this would arrange things so I would get off first in Shibuya. Decided not to do it, just to deal with the consequences in Shibs as they may occur.

I was third off the train behind two tied for first place, but first to the top of the stairs, zipping through the gaps of slow people. Shibuya was pretty crowded; I was necessarily slowed down by the mid-sized crowds crowding the gates and ticket machine area.
