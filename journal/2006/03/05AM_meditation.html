8:01am MST Sunday 05 March 2006

I woke up without an alarm around 5am, flopped around in bed a bit and then decided to practice meditation for an hour. I did 35 minutes before I just couldn't anymore; several times I nearly gave up; several times I found myself falling asleep. I was using the ceiling tiles and plumbing supports as physical reference points to monitor my posture. If I couldn't feel them on my left shoulder and right temple, then I knew I had slouched.

Plus, the ship's rocking keeps me from being able to sit in an effortlessly balanced way.
