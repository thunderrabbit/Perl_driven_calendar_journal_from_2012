11:15am ChST Wednesday 22 March 2006 (day 13146)

Totally surprised and delighted to get a note from Tomoe today; I happened to pass her in the stairs, and said HI like I have every time I've seen her cause she's just so... Tomoe and smiley and cool, and from Okinawa... but this time she was like, "I have a letter for you," (!) and I was like, "wow!" and she asked if I'd be interested in walking around with her in port tomorrow.

Boy, would I ever!

I could hardly believe my "luck" ... did she mean with a bunch of her friends? Did she plan to buy a big TV and need someone to help carry it to the ship?

I took the note up to the Theater, where I planned to work on my speech, and sat meditatively, feeling the happiness flowing through my body, watching for jumps to conclusions, and noticing differences in how I thought about Tomoe pre and post invitation.

I tried to identify quite carefully moments when my thinking turned from walking around with Tomoe to the possibility of kissing Tomoe, and thinking back to the time when I first met her and learned that she's only like 22 or something (I just remember putting a "too young" tag on her; she could be as old as 27 and get that), and I noted that her English is not exactly money.

Thought back to _If the Buddha Dated_, and noted that I'm just in the stage of "it's okay to chat with this person" (or some such; that's my clumsy wording, not the book's) and not supposed to try chewing on her or anything.

Once I felt basically centered, I opened the letter, slowly, noting that I didn't even know if it would actually be addressed to *me* as opposed to a random invite.

It was to me, asking to walk around in port tomorrow or on top deck some night. That means kissing, in my mind. Yikes stripes!
