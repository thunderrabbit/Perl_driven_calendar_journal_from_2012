8:12pm ANAT Saturday 18 March 2006 (day 13142)

Today, by semi-jokingly volunteering to be the Chief of the GET "tribe," I ended up being the spokesperson for the Shizuo, who was nominated as Chief. At the cultural exchange, I was given a place of honor, seated opposite the actual chief of the village who hosted us as everyone did their dances and such.

They served us cava, which is basically a mixture of dried peppers (not spicy) and water, which can be prepared to the desired strength by using more or less water.
