10:36pm CST Friday 03 March 2006

Chowed in Yacht Club once we got back to the ship; Akane came over for our lesson (which I had forgotten). We chatted a while and she worked out my birthdate given my current days old date, and pulled out the three splinters.

She told me stories about growing up; she was quite a tomboy!

She will be 10,000 days old on 17 September 2011. She marveled at the idea of being that old, and I told her the story of having wondered where I would be in 10 years while gazing out over Houston traffic, then finding that journal entry almost exactly 10 years later. She will probably write a letter to her future self, one for her 10,000th day, and one for 10 years from now.
