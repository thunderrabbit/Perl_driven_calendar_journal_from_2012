(transcribed from Book Two of my diary)

12:15AM Monday August 31 1992

"Today" I read the first chapter in each of my books. That was my first homework blockade. I went to the school to hang out and I saw Caleb walking to his room with Audrey, the girl who did the shrill giggle sound file on the NeXT, and his roommate. I went with them to check out Caleb's new computer. While I played on it, Caleb and Audrey were totally kissing. I didn't pay attention too much until they sang some lines from Rocky Horror Picture Show. A bit later, I noted that Audrey was totally kissing Caleb's roommate. Dude.
