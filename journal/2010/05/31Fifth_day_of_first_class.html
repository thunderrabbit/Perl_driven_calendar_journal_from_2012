I just about walked out of my Thai language class because he was going way too quickly for me. I mean, he *has* been going way to quickly for me. Then I was like, aight stop to myself until I could figure out what to do. Then to him I was like, "aight stop," and explained, "I can't read these characters yet," referring to all the Thai characters that look a lot like แตวดาสหปรอป๔๓ย๒งกงด๘๙รสคลาหม๗รปกง to a non-Thai reader like me.

I go, "I just want to work on reading," so I asked him to leave all the stuff he had written on the board, and I began to work out what I could read / recognize, etc.

Now, though I can't yet write them with my physical keyboard, I can read and write stuff like, หก, สาม and สิบ., written with my electronic keyboard.

Class went great once I got him working the way I wanted.
