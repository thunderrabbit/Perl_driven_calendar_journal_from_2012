7:32pm ICT Saturday 8 May 2010 (day 14654)

Great first day in Chiang Mai!

I successfully walked from the airport to my guest house, booked a room for four nights for 600 Bhat total, chatted a bit with the peeps there, and then walked to Sunshine Massage School to ensure I can find it on Monday.

Talked with founder-owner-manager Max a bit and generally looks like things will be great. He gave me water, good advice on getting an education visa, and a list of nearerby accomodation. On the list was ImmEco, which was my first choice when I searched online, but they gave either a null or negative response via email. Max suggested that was cause I had asked during a busy season, but now they were probably quite available, especially with so many countries telling their citizens to NOT travel to Thailand because of the riots.

What riots?

I walked over to Imm Eco, fell in love with its gardens, signed up for a month lodging including free breakfast for 10,000 Bhat. Sweet. I move in tomorrow.
