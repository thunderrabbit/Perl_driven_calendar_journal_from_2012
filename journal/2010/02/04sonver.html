Hung out with Joanna, Tallulah and Mike tonight in Kichijoji after Joanna and Tallulah's last performance in Japan.

Mike's got a lot of interesting stories! He's been in Japan for a while. He was on Nijima when Mijima blew up 10 years ago and stayed when people were evacuated until it got to the point they were evacuated by helicopter after 3 days at a house that had been scheduled to host 40 people for 10 days. Empty beach, plenty of food, plenty of volcano to watch. Awesome!

Tallulah was telling me one of her favorite songs by NIN is "A Warm Place" That's on the album Broken, I think. Right?

She's halfway from Sydney, so I asked if she knew Anikiko. Nope. I guess Sydney is bigger than small enough for all musicians to know each other.

Oh, very interestingly, when I was telling her why I had come to Japan, and that I had found PB and sorta stayed for that reason, she goes, "are you Buddhist?" and I was like, "wha?" because how the heck did she discern that?
