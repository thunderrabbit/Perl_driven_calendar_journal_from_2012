Though one might quickly think the logbook of names is a waste, one might later realize it gives one benefit. When writing our names, we write the reason for coming to the immigration office, so when the guy gets to our name, he knows which thai-scripted button to press on the machine, thereby putting us in different numbered queues, which allows individual counter agents to focus on only certain types of paperwork requests.
