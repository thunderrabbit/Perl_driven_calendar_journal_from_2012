Climbing a really big tree

In a hotel, I went from my room directly into the onsen area, and was there about 10 minutes when Fred came in from a tough day at school. He asked me about my day and I told him, but I didn't ask him about his day until he offered. Turns out he had had a rough time on the school bus, with its new type of seat belt, that was more like a cage than a seat belt. "They could see I had been crying, " he said, " so they put me into the seats near the driver, and they are seats with a gate in front that ensures people stay there. It was fun at first, but a few minutes down the road and I realized it hurt."

I checked the clock, and it was 4:50pm. I hadn't told the clerk what time I started, but decided it would be easier to tell the clerk we had started at 4:10, than to try to explain I started at 4:10 and Fred had just arrived. Went into the room and found the reception desk for the onsen and was trying to speak Japanese, "すみません、あの、１６時１０分から始まりましたんだけどう。" and I woke up.
