On the way there and back, I rode the train with David; we talked about upcoming societal changes, including economy and "2012" which I basically said I had heard three general things:

  1. "It" is already happening, a gradual change in humanity's consciousness focused around 2012.

  2. The planet is traveling through a portion of the universe that's more energetic; it will push everyone forward in their personal growth. For people to whom this is a new concept, it will be hell. For people who've been doing their work and have a framework with which they can understand their experiences, it will be more bearable / fine

  3. Every N00,000 years, the continents begin to shift around at the pace of a human walking. Not fast enough to cause tsunami the size of Eiffel Tower, but fast enough that people will go, "d'oh!"

He said a friend of his said a big change is coming soon. I guessed either

  1. official disclosure that our governments have been chatting with extraterrestrials

  2. free energy machines will be released

  3. economic collapse and chaos

Hopefully the second one. I think that would be best for us. Seems like that would let humans take care of our own problems. The first option seems more like "come save us!" and the last option would probably lead to us taking care of ourselves, but with a bloody revolution in the interim.
