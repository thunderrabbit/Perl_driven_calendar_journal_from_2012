4:48pm ICT Tuesday 29 June 2010 (day 14706)

Upon arrival at Sinshai's place, I noted quickly that my bear Johnny was no longer riding cutely on the back of my bicycle. (He wasn't riding un-cutely either.) "Oh no," and I headed back the way I had come. I guess the springy thing just wasn't strong enough to hold him, and he jumped out when I jumped up a curb.

I rolled slowly along the moat, looking for a soaked bear floating facedown, and eventually found him, facedown, but not floating. It appeared he was carefully examining the grass. I didn't take a picture because I didn't have my camera handy, plus the shot can be recreated at any time (like I ever will).
