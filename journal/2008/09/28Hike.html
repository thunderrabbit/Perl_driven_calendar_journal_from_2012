Now near the bottom of the hill, or near enough that I can hear traffic, and am currently sitting with my legs dangling off the edge of a little access road for maintenence guys to work on a tower. (I saw them.)

To get here, I zoomed down the mountain ahead of my three cohorts in climbing (and descending). I had been playing a game of closing my eyes and waiting for them to go down a ways, and then zipping down to the end of the line where I closed my eyes again to wait. I realized I needed to get in front so I could do some unbridled zooming, so I requested that and started to zoom.

I'm gonna guess it will take them until 4:20 to get down, because I'm guessing I went down for 5 minutes and guessing about 4 times faster than they were descending.

Got a couple of pictures overlooking Gora station: one with us in the way, and one without. I hope I can put them on my website in a way that shows where we were.

While I played my game, Helma and I were quiet, and Travis was helping Tomoko with questions about sendmail and a different program.

From here, we're planning to find an onsen in which we can bathe our muscles, and then head home, probably after some foodage.
