On the train last night a little kid got on and was determined to find a window so he culd watch out the back of the train. He din't find a space available, so I relinquished my space for him. He said clearly in English, "thank you!" and i was like, "sure!" As I got off the train in Musashi-Kosugi, he goes, "see you!" and I mirrored his enthusiasm, "see you!" and he came to the side window to wave bye to me. I matched his wave and waved bigger as the train rolled away, finally using my umbrella as a wave extension, though he may have been too far away to see it. In any case, I made a new friend. I'll be amazed if I ever see him again.
