Here's the overview of my first Castle of Dreams story, perhaps to be written as a book:

  1. Two kids (*) go into the cave, and one gets his arm chawed off by a firebat.

  2. Scout Seven happened to be there and save the kid, save for his arm.

  3. Sebastian / the spooky guy from the basement / Finneous (sp) try to pass legislation to keep people from going back there.

  4. Scout Seven and the other kid go back to the cave, with Stanley Steamer there to protect them from firebats.

  5. Firebats attack like crazy. They get out through an unknown exit.

  6. Sebastian et. al. take the kid and Scout Seven to court for violating their law.

  7. Finneous is called to the stand. He gives his sob story; the court case turns upside down and is thrown out.

  8. The real truth is revealed.

12:03pm JST

Hmm. That was easy. That means I'll need to trim a lot of what I wrote during the 2007 NaNoWriMo, but those can be saved for other stories.

(*) Because I say "two kids," though I gave them names when I wrote during the 2007 NaNoWriMo, I don't remember their names now. I know from reading the preface to Ender's Game (I think), that therefore they aren't real characters. "I couldn't remember her children's names."

I'm seeing these more as short stories... Dang this is perfect for the Castle of Dreams wiki; I can write them as stories in the library, and use the navigation engine to flip through the chapters of the story.
