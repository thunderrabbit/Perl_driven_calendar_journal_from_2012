Confronted my post office nemesis when sending the card to Ayse's mom. I made the mistake of thinking there was kanji for the Japanese word for Turkey. So I didn't write it on the card; I was just going to ask them the kanji and write it down.

So when she saw the card, she was like blah blah blah, meaning "where is this going?"

And I go 「トルコ」

And she goes, 「東京？」(*)

Um, no. 「トルコ。」and I tried some other variants that couldn't be right, and then was like, 「英語で"Turkey"」

And she was like, "______" with a blank look, so I came from a different angle.

イッスタンブルと言う町のくにはなんとういう？」

More blank looks, but she turned around and asked the group, who also didn't know.

One of them did think of it and was like, 「トルコ」

And I was like, 「あぁ、だからトルコと言ってた。」

She looked it up in the book and I saw the カタカナ. Dammit. There's no kanji; it's just トルコ。

(*) I can appreciate on one level that she at least understood I might be saying Tokyo.
