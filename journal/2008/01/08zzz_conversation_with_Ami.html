Last night up until 2am; way later than I wanted. Ami happened to be in Nakameguro when I finally headed home around 10.5pm, so I stopped by the bar (her friend Lupan's) where she was chillin'. Told her about the email from and to Nat and didn't quite get the response I had predicted.

Ami seemed to see it as the end of our relationship because I fall in love with girls all the time (and back out just as quickly). Basically Ami was like, "I'll be worried for the 3 months that you're gone."

I had (though couldn't necessarily explain) several responses:

  1. don't worry; be happy

  2. I don't even know if I'll be accepted

  3. the voyage wouldn't be until a year from now

  4. I can stay faithful to you for the 3.5 months.

  5. I want you to want me to chase my dreams

  6. all we have is *now*; so be happy now.

It took 2.5 hours to go through the process, most of which was done standing by her bicycle near Hirama station, after which my right knee was a bit stiff and felt swollen on the inside, but I couldn't feel anything weird on the outside.
