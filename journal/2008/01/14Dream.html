I had a heavy crystal tube given to me by janette and filled it with oysters. There was a cat trying to get a snack, so I set the tube outside and it began to roll away and about to drop off a step onto a brick surface, so I was afraid for a bit that it would break. I went out to get it, but I was just too late. It hit the brick surface with a thunk, and the I picked it up, dumped out the oysters and brought it inside to wash.

There were still a couple sliced mushroms stuck to the inside of the tube. As I washed them out, I noticed a little chip on one end of the tube, and a matching one on the other end. I hoped janette was too fond of this tube, and I woke up.
