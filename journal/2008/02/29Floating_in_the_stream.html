This past weekend, after/during some surprisingly intense winds, the stream near my house was filled with more leaves and rubbish than normal. There's a particular point where three flat-topped rocks sit in a semi-random looking triangle, plus a slight widening of the stream around them. I bet these were placed as a bridge across the stream, although now the stream has been embanked and blocked with a fence so only fence climbers can get in.

On occasion, this triangle of rocks catches an errant stick, which catches a leaf, which catches something else, until, like a pyramid scheme, there's a whole slew of bits and pieces stuck behind these rocks.

I decided to take a picture each morning, and put them into some sort of ... something so they can be viewed like a slideshow or a movie. I've only got like 5 days worth so far, and after two days of clearish waters, today was a big cloggy day.
