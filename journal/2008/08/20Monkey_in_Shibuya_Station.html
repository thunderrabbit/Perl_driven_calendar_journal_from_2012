I couldn't help but notice a large cordron of the exit gates blocked off with ropes, and cops hollering for everyone to keep moving. Some larger sized video cameras were on tripods; I whipped out my camera for a few snaps and then switched to video mode as I walked around the perimter of the circle. As I got around to the other side, I saw a monkey chillin' (?) up on the sign to the Tokyu Toyoko Line.

Oh dear. Poor monkey! I wonder from where s/he came... I didn't see anyone who looked like a concerned owner. But I did see a guy with a fishing net, like a butterfly net. I doubt that would be a polite way to catch such a critter. If it were up to me, I'd climb up the ladder with some snacks, and just start munching until the monkey joined me. Then cuddle my new monkey friend all the way to fame and fortune.
