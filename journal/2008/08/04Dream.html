1:15am JST Monday 4 August 2008 (day 14012)

Hang gliding from the top of Mt Fuji wasn't as easy as I had hoped, but if my om had done it, I bet I could; I had to get another jacket though; on my first trial flight it was a bit cold. I didn't want to do a full flight, but just a test, so I was lucky I could bring the kite back down after I took off from the top. I came down only like 30 meters later, and then went quickly to the bottom to get another shirt; I was looking for a tank top with high contrast against the snow, but couldn't find one before I woke up.
