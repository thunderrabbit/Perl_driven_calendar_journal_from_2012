9:19am JST Monday 4 August 2008 (day 14012)

With the enthusiasm of this weekend workshop, I was able to drag myself to meditation position and practice meditation first thing this morning. And, I had another breakthrough:

Previously, I would get up to meditation position, and then about 2 minutes into it, feel shockingly tired and give up, lie back down and sleep.

But today, I got up to meditation position, and about two minutes into it, I felt the subtle tingles that I described previously as shocking tiredness. But instead of succumbing to them, I felt them. At one point I was like, what is that? and they vanished. So then I re-relaxed my outer mind and noticed they came back. And they could be perceived as crazy tiredness, but I stayed with the feeling, and stayed with the feeling, and lost consciousness for a bit, then came back, and focused on breathing (with throat friction).. etc.

Basically I passed through a mental block to meditating, and experienced some energy in a different way than previously.
