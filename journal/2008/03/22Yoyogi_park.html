After taking my mac to the Apple Store, I got some lunch and ate by a fountain. I took a semi funy picture there. I miss Francois.

Closer to Yoyogi Park, I looked for Francois perhaps being sold by one of the junk vendors. I came across a funny old man doing magic; I couldn't figure out how he was manifesting coins from side to side, but then he showed me how to do it.

Looked for Fuse the contact juggler, but didn't find him.

I did, however, find a bunch of dudes drumming. I meditatively sat near them both before and after looking for Fuse. I gave 1000 yen to the genkiest guy in the group; his hands were tough and smooth like well worn leather. As soon as I gave him the cash he suggested it would be better if we all go out drinking together; and I declined so he gave the money to another guy nearby, for the group.
