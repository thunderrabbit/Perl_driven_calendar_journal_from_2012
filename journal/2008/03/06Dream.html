Floating in the ocean, watching some boats jump ramps, the first boat was a light white rubber raft with dual motors, one of which had shifted over to the right, so it went all sideways in the air; we tried to get it balanced out to jump again, but a van like Mr Van (*) was ready to jump. It had a great jump and landed upside down, and then righted itself like a duck and was floating perfectly on its four wheels. I thought that was way cool, but no one else seemed impressed.
