Francois came back, and I was so happy to be holding him again. I spent a good deal of time just looking into his little plastic eyes because I knew it was a dream and I wouldn't be able to keep him when I woke up.

Then I was in a cafe where the cashier said, "oh, someone wrote a note about your rabbit," and I was like, "that's great!" but she didn't still have the note anymore, and couldn't remember the contents, except that Francois would be returned after the Olympics. I pulled a five dollar bill out of my wallet and gave it to her, saying, "next time, keep the note and give it to me."

I was at the bar drinking a coke and a little boy about 4 years old came up and put his straw into my drink and claimed it as his own. I looked at the drink and started laughing my ass off because he had also put a tea bag into it, with the basic plan of "grossing me out," so I wouldn't want it anymore. I was more grossed out that I was drinking coke in the first place than some kid put tea into it. His mom came up and apologized, but I told her he was a genius and would do well in life.
