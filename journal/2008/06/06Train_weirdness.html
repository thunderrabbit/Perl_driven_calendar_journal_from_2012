8:13pm JST Friday 6 June 2008 (day 13953)

I suddenly lurched awake as the train jerked to an emergency stop. I looked up and saw the next station was Hiyoshi; I had slept through Motosumiyoshi.

The conductor came onto the intercom all, "uh, yeah, there's a red light, but we're going to go to Shin Maruko."

"red light" is my translation for しんごる (sp), which for a long time I thought was a Japanese version of "signal" but is actually a Japanese word.

Anyway, I was like, "what?" cause we passed Shin Maruko; it's the third station back from where we were! No one else seemed to notice/care that we would be going *backwards* on the line for a bit to get there.

Then he changed his story, "oh, we're going to Hiyoshi," and I was much less surprised, and everyone else seemed to be equally disinterested.
