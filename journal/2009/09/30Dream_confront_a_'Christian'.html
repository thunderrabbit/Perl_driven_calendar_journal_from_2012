In a house, with some commotion going on outside, and two guys knocked on the door and sorta let themselves in; no one else inside really knew how to handle them, so they asked me to so I went to the main guy all, "get OUT," while pointing at the door as I bounced him out of the room.

"Now," I said, once we were outside, "it may not seem like a very friendly way to greet somebody," we chuckled, "but I feel uncomfortable when people I don't know barge into the house. How may I help you?"

"Well, I just wondered if you had found Jesus Christ as your personal Lord and Sav..."

"Um, yeah, about that. I am pretty much clear that I don't subscribe to the Christian mythology and religious silliness, so don't waste your time."

"I know how you feel, and I used to think the same thing.. that was until I got married and found Jesus and have been Right ever since."

I thought for a moment and realized things didn't quite add up. "So you mean you were married *before* you found Christ? And you call yourself a Christian?" (*)

He looked sheepish, and his friends all looked at him.

I continued, "and you call yourself Christian, but go barging into another man's house without asking?"

He looked down at his feet and I woke up.

(*) Yes, I realize this dream-logic makes no sense in our waking reality.
