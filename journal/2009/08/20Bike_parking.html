I felt a bit embarrassed going in to bike parking, so I unnecessarily asked "where is parking?" and the guy goes "yes," and I go, "where is parking?" (a bit passive-aggressively) and he goes, "yes, ah, 3rd floor."

The above is in English, but the actual conversation was in Japanese. On my way up the ramp, I was thinking, why would he answer yes to my first question? and realized he could have thought I asked, "anywhere is parking?" and the correct answer would be "yes," so it made sense.

The conversation was actually

「どこでいいですか。」

「はい。」

「どこでいいですか。」

「はい。あっ、三階です。」

I think that's the correct kanji for 'さんかい' meaning 3F, or 3rd floor.
