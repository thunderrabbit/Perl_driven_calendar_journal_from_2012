I searched for "mac games" on Google and came upon a site called thriftmac, and selected several games whose descriptions sounded more like puzzle games than 1st person shooters.

In that list were some duds, and one diamond with a dud name: "Krank"

    The name "krank" is probably the last I would pick for this beautiful
    game. Perhaps "spheres" or "gentle dreamland" would be more appropriate.

    Super-kudos to the authors for making a meditative game that rewards
    patience and gentle game play.

    The graphics are beautiful and creative, with subtle clues describing 
    how to play.

    A lovely soundtrack with customized gameplay sounds to match each 
    level deserves five of five stars.

Download krank on Sourceforge.
