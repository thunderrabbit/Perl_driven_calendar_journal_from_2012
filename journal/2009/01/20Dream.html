In a room, where perhaps I had been meditating, and a guru came in. He looked like an American. I told him I was interested in having a guru, so that I could have the experience of oneness with god. I was like, "yeah, I've done it a couple of times, but not consistently,..." and then I realized the truth that having done it *at all*, with no guru instruction, was wonderfully beyond imagination and I just started to cry.

Crying crying for a moment, while the guru looked on with compassion, saying nothing and letting the fullness of the moment complete itself.

Wow. What a cool dream. I haven't consciously recalled oneness with God, but perhaps this is an interesting indication.
