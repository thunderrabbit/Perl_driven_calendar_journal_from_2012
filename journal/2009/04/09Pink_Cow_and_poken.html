Actually I'm writing about yesterday today.

I went to visit Traci at The Pink Cow at 5pm, ate a delicious chicken burrito while helping her with website stuff a bit.. Not sure that I helped *all* that much, but, I also bought a poken from her.

It's a cute little plastic panda with a USB port on one side, and a IR chip on the other. Get a couple pokens to give a high four, and you've just taken part in the latest way to exchange business cards. Web 2.0 meets cute and namecards all at once. And it's just hit Japan!

I'm sure this will be all the rage soon, so I got one. I chose the panda, though a few other designs were available, with more to come, I'm sure.
