Last night I let Krank play and play by itself on Easy Level 27. It didn't remove any of the stones in a 6 hour period. I guess it's more rare than I had hoped. (basically requires 4 of 6 same colored stones to arrive at a certain spot on the field before any of the other 18 other-color bubbles arrive in the same spot..) There are four spots, each with its desire to get 4 same-colored bubbles. The bubbles are literally little circles that move around the screen, bouncing off the sides and each other in a nicely chaotic way. They move nearly frictionlessly, only losing a bit of energy when they bounce off the sides of the screen. But then gaining a lot of energy after four bubbles collect on the hot spots, and then are exploded away at high speeds.

Anyway, a couple days ago, after a few hours of auto-play, one of the hotspots had apparently received exactly 4 of its color bubbles and was removed from the screen. I thought an overnight adventure might do even better.
