I just got off the phone with Sally while driving home to Dallas from San Antonio after the youth advisor training.

So great to confirm profound robust everlasting love for one another, even as she's dating a local guy named Rick who she's known for a while.

That's why I seemed a bit distracted while saying you are a beautiful girl in Russian.
