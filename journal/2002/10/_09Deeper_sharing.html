2:45pm EDT Wednesday 9 October 2002

Abbey came home after lunch and I was sitting lumpily by her bed. She asked how I was. "weird" and "are you ready for deeper sharing?" I paused; I didn't know if I was ready.

I told her the whole Marta situation as it stands so far, and she held me from the side as we were both sitting against her bed. I didn't belittle Marta nor her feelings nor experiences. I do want what's best for the youth. The thing that tears me up most is the idea of not attending Houston 2003; the youth are planning to name "Rah Rah Rob Rally" or some such. I am so honored, and how stinky would it be if I couldn't be there??

As of yet, I have not documented everything that has happened in the past week with Marta. I've gotten some emails from her and sent some back. Right now it looks like I won't go to San Antonio, but I really want to go to Houston.
