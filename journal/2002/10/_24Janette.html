10:35am CDT Thursday 24 October 2002

Earlier this morning when Janette and I greeted on another, I felt really emotionally poopy and just wanted to hold connect possess her. I went for a while just following her around wanting a hug, but being afraid(?) to ask her or something silly like that. I finally did hug her and just cried and said I felt partially like I shouldn't be here anymore. Just so sad. Then cried more on my bed and then she came in and hugged me some and asked if I could remove a branch from the electrical wire coming into her house. I did.

I just wanted us to be where everything is funny again. I don't want to break up with her.
