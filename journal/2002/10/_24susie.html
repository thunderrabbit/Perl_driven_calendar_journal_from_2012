9:39pm CDT Thursday 24 October 2002

Just talked to Susie Robinson. I told her the whole me and Marta story and asked if she thought it would be beneficial to the youth if I did not attend Houston Rally. She thought that would slight the youth and that they have been expecting this opportunity to say goodbye to me since I've been so integral to rallies for a long time (i.e. longer than the entire span of the current set of YRUUers)

I asked her how we can pacify Marta and Susie said she'll talk to Leigh Lyles who can talk to Marta and maybe help her be okay with it or not go or something.

Susie basically agrees with me that it's not a youth issue and should not affect my attendance at Houston. Marta doesn't have any say over whether or not I go.

Susie does agree that it's probably best that I don't go to San Antonio, and though it sucks not to go, it's a relatively small price to pay. I agree with that.

I'm thankful for Susie's willingness to talk to Leigh. Susie and I trust that this will all work out for the best.
