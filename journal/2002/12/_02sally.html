11:38pm PST Monday 2 December 2002

So Sally and I had a great time today. I really enjoyed being near her and talking and running around the campus with her. She happened to see me as I walked from the library in search of the Kinko's. We went to lunch together and sat with her friend Stefan and his friend someone.

She held my hand or my arm while we walked; huge smiles everywhere we went.

I'm so in love with her.

I imagine she is a bit torn right now. Either that or she is lying her ass off, which I don't think she would do to herself. She really likes being around me and having me nearby, but she's officially the girlfriend of Rick, who she recognizes as immature and not ultimately The One for her.

"Take care of yourself; I can imagine this [you paying attention to two guys] is difficult on you."

She agreed. And I presume appreciates that I can tell.

I told her "never settle for anything but the best. even if it's not me, never settle for anything but the best. although I am the best.." just as he was dinging her doorbell and we had been huge hugging our last huge hugs we'd have for some hours while he came to visit and we watched part of Blazing Saddles and most of History of the World, two movies which I find rather silly, with a few funny parts.

Sally and I enjoyed Y Tu Mama Tambien a lot. I don't think Rick did as much.

As Rick was preparing to leave, Sally carefully examined a small carved wooden statue, lost in deep thought. I will inquire what's going on in her mind.

During Y Tu Mama, in the scene where all 3 of them were doing it, I was thinking, "you guys wanna do that?" and started laughing silently to myself. Sally was like, "are you okay???" knowing that I was fine.
