I'm in Norman Oklahom now at Kris Scott's house and surprised to hear my phone ring twice in succession. I got out of bed and answered it through the pocket of my shorts. It was Marta in tears.

Sorry to wake me and doesn't want to hate me and wants me to come back and is sorry to ask and hurts so bad and was afraid to ask anything of me.

She wants me to delay my trip by two weeks, which amounts to 2/5ths of my remaining time. No way no way am I doing that in the name of friendship with someone that I'm hardly friends with. I mean fuckin' Maggie wouldn't ask me for that kind of committment from me, and she's my best friend in the planet.

I'm not sure what Marta is going through, but we probably could have danced around this bullshit spiderweb for hours but God turned off my phone for me just in time for me to say my battery is low. I'll write to her when Kris is in school, but I'm not going to come back to Denton for two weeks.
