2:45pm CDT Tuesday 10 September 2002

Marta is crying on the couch. She's scared about me leaving for six days. She's scared that she has grown so dependent on someone being in her life so quickly. That scares me too.

I told her that she has this opportunity to reclaim her space and her power and to take care of herself. That she could skip the week of school and still be fine cause she is so smart. Or that she could choose to attend all the classes because she is so strong.

How is it that one woman has been reduced to tears over not having a baby that she never wanted and never really had? Why is she so crushed by this situation?

She's reaching out to someone on the phone as I type this. I'm glad for her for that.

I'm going to the Bibby's house to get out of Marta's hair and to see Mattboy and Joel as they begin their big adventure around the US.

So great that Marta is crying on the phone to someone. She has a support system if she will only see it and use it.
