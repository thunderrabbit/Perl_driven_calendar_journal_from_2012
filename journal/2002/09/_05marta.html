9:04am CDT Thursday 5 September 2002

Soon after I wrote the dream entry, I was all, "oh yeah, I wrote a list of things to do today," and I set out to Kinko's to get several of them done. Marta noticed me leaving unexpectedly and asked if there is anything she has done. I was all, "no; I've just been feeling unproductive lately.."

And the conversation developed to blatant honesty and it was wonderful. I had trapped myself in her apartment/life and felt like I wasn't doing anything for myself and she felt like I was abandoning her (pretty much that is true) and as my lover/miscarriage partner wants to ask me to stay until she bleeds this out, and as my friend she really doesn't want to keep me from my trip.

Lots of awesome tears and getting all this shit out into the open. I told her I feel scared every time we have sex. Scared both of re-impregnating her and scared of emotional bondage. I asked that we no longer have sex and I wonder how long that will last. I told her a bit more about my sex addiction and what that means - that the addict is always trying to have sex and I forgot to tell her that the relationship ends up being with the addiction and not with the person apparently involved (in this case her).

I'm afraid of relationship and committment and that there will be some new other perfect person around the corner. I want to live my Life and find my Self and I know the irony is that my Life and my Self are right here with me wherever I am.

She wondered if I need to sow my wild oats while I'm on my Adventure or if I need to just be non-sexual. I don't have an answer to that question.

She just feels alone and scared during this non-bleeding moment; she doesn't know what to expect when she's no longer expecting. She has asked the simple favor of me to stay with her until the embryo and all its blood have passed.

Part of me is like, "fuck; I'm delaying my trip more," which sounds a lot like, "I feel like I wasted my whole day," which is what I said to Rachelle after I paid for her abortion, when what I really wanted to say is, "that experience really sucked; I hope I don't have to do it again."

- - - -

Marta really prides herself on being strong and independent so it's really scary and different for her to (aw fuck I didn't think about how helpful it would be if I had driven her to school) want my support through this process. I totally understand that.

10:19am

So I have basically decided to stay with Marta until she bleeds. In a beatiful showing of universal love and synchronicity, she had some blood expulsion on her next visit to the restroom.

2:40pm

I visited Kinko's and visited some websites about miscarriages and what to expect and such. I left the browser windows open for Marta to read them when I got back. I actually didn't read much/any of them, but just got them for her.

She cried a lot while reading them and I pretty much don't really know how to support her, but just to let her cry and stuff. She put some water on to boil to cook noodles before I arrived home and I finished cooking them and helped Marta just be able to sit on the couch and read and cry.

Ate noodles with cheese and now are in bed; she slept some and snored a bit, and i read a bunch of livejournal entries of YRUUers. I'm a bit envious that they just write whatever the fuck they want (or so it seems to me) and I censor my writing since I know they will be reading it. I have these private entries but part of me wants to make it all public. I think a lot of people could benefit if we all were a bit more honest and open about feelings and experiences and shit. But I'm scared to start. Thank god for Eminem who isn't scared to express it publicly.

He's like a trailblazer, or a, um, pioneer. (thanks Marta for the word)

I'm going to go get Janette from DFW in a few hours and drop her off at her house and then come back to hang with Marta. It's weird to tell Joey and Jack and Janette I'm going to Marta's and not tell them why I'm going to Marta's. But they can come to their own conclusions.
