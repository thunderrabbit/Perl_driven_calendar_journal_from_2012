12:20pm Monday 10 June 2002

Last night I called Bruce but he never called me back. I have decided to not do it again.

Just a few minutes ago I got a call on my cell phone from number 713-498-3363. The obviously female voice said her name was Amina, and asked if I was available.

"Available for what?"

"For employment."

For an instant, I thought it was maybe legit, but then I realized I haven't put my number out anywhere asking for random employment. "Doing what?" I asked.

"Are you a sex ___?" she asked. I couldn't make out the second word.

"No I'm not."

"Okay, sorry; bye."

Now I'm all wondering what was being proposed. What would it have paid? Where would it be? Was it a police setup? All these questions...

And I'm pondering the philosophical questions based on the story of the man who wouldn't accept help from the boat, the raft, nor the helicopter as flood waters overtook his home, trusting that God would take care of him. The guy drowned and God said, "I sent a boat, a raft, and a helicopter; what else did you want?"

Was this my big break in making some big bucks fast? I have been prayerful and trusting that the infinite wealth of the universe is available to me; maybe this is part of the response.
